## How close are we to a vision for 2010?

我们离 2010 年的愿景有多近？

Twenty five years ago today, the EU's [IST advisory group](https://cordis.europa.eu/article/id/14054-ist-advisory-group-istag-takes-strategic-approach) published a paper about the future of "Ambient Intelligence". Way before the world got distracted with cryptoscams and AI slop, we genuinely thought that computers would be so pervasive and well-integrated that the dream of "[Ubiquitous Computing](https://shkspr.mobi/blog/2002/04/disappearing-computer-2002/)" would become a reality.

25 年前的今天，欧盟的[IST 顾问组](https://cordis.europa.eu/article/id/14054-ist-advisory-group-istag-takes-strategic-approach)发布了一篇关于"环境智能"未来的论文。早在世界被加密货币骗局和 AI 垃圾分散注意力之前，我们真的认为计算机会如此普及和良好集成，以至于"[普适计算](https://shkspr.mobi/blog/2002/04/disappearing-computer-2002/)"的梦想会成为现实。

The ISTAG published an optimistic paper called "[Scenarios for ambient intelligence in 2010](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/262007900_Scenarios_for_ambient_intelligence_in_2010)". It's a brilliant look at what the future might have been. Let's go through some of the scenarios and see how close 2026 is to 2000's vision of 2010.

ISTAG 发表了一篇名为"[2010 年环境智能情景](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/262007900_Scenarios_for_ambient_intelligence_in_2010)"的乐观论文。这是对可能存在的未来的精彩展望。让我们浏览一些情景，看看 2026 年与 2000 年对 2010 年的愿景有多接近。

## Scenario 1: 'Maria' – Road Warrior (close-term future)

情景 1：'Maria'——公路勇士（近期未来）

Our titular heroine steps off a long haul flight into a foreign country.

我们的女主人公走出长途航班，进入一个外国。

she knows that she can travel much lighter than less than a decade ago, when she had to carry a collection of different so-called personal computing devices (laptop PC, mobile phone, electronic organisers and sometimes beamers and printers). Her computing system for this trip is reduced to one highly personalised communications device, her 'P–Com' that she wears on her wrist.

她知道她可以比不到十年前旅行得更轻便，当时她必须携带一系列不同的所谓个人计算设备（笔记本电脑、移动电话、电子记事本，有时还有投影仪和打印机）。她这次旅行的计算系统简化为一个高度个性化的通信设备，她的'P-Com'，戴在手腕上。

Well… OK! Not a bad start. You probably wouldn't want everything controlled by your smart watch - but the mobile is a good substitute.

嗯……好吧！不错的开始。你可能不会想让所有东西都由智能手表控制——但手机是一个很好的替代品。

she is able to stroll through immigration without stopping because her P-Comm is dealing with the ID checks as she walks.

她能够在不停下脚步的情况下走过移民局，因为她的 P-Comm 在她行走时处理身份检查。

We're getting closer to digital ID. But outside of a few experiments, there's no international consensus. However, every modern passport has an NFC chip which can be read by most airports. You still need to hold your passport on the reader, but it's usually quicker than queuing for a human.

我们正在接近数字身份。但除了一些实验之外，没有国际共识。然而，每本现代护照都有一个 NFC 芯片，可以被大多数机场读取。你仍然需要将护照放在读取器上，但这通常比排队等人工检查要快。

Maria heads to her rented car:

Maria 走向她租的车：

The car opens as she approaches. It starts at the press of a button: she doesn't need a key. She still has to drive the car but she is supported in her journey downtown to the conference centre-hotel by the traffic guidance system

车在她接近时打开。按一下按钮就启动了：她不需要钥匙。她仍然必须开车，但交通引导系统支持她前往市中心的会议中心酒店

Lots of cars now have wireless entry and are button controlled. Rental cars often have mobile app unlocking.

现在很多车都有无线进入功能，是按钮控制的。租车通常有手机应用解锁。

## Scenario 2: 'Dimitrios' – Relaxing at home

情景 2：'Dimitrios'——在家放松

Dimitrios is at home. He can look at his 'wallpaper' TV - which really looks like framed wallpaper until it is activated.

Dimitrios 在家。他可以看到他的'壁纸'电视——在它被激活之前真的看起来像带框的壁纸。

Ha! Still waiting on that one. Flexible displays exist but are incredibly expensive and rare. It's cheaper and easier to disguise a TV as a picture frame. But even that is pretty rare.

哈！还在等那个。柔性显示器存在，但非常昂贵和罕见。把电视伪装成相框更便宜也更容易。但即使那样也相当罕见。

He can gesture at the wall to change the view - perhaps to check who is at the front door, or to see if the wine he put in the outdoor chiller is ready.

他可以对着墙做手势来改变视图——也许是查看谁在门前，或者看看他放在户外冷却器里的酒是否准备好了。

Nope. Gesture control is still fairly niche.

不。手势控制仍然相当小众。

**批判性思考评论：**

这篇文章是对技术预测准确性的有趣回顾。2000 年欧盟顾问组对 2010 年"环境智能"的愿景在 2026 年看来，既有命中也有偏差。

预测准确的领域：
- **设备融合**：智能手机确实取代了多种设备（相机、音乐播放器、GPS、手电筒等）
- **无线连接**：蓝牙和 NFC 确实实现了无缝设备交互
- **数字身份**：护照 NFC 芯片确实简化了边境检查

预测偏差的领域：
- **个人代理**："个人代理自动谈判酒店和租车"仍然不存在
- **手势控制**：大多数场景下仍然小众，语音和触摸仍是主流
- **环境计算**：设备并没有"消失"到环境中，而是集中在手机上

更深层的问题：为什么某些预测会失败？
1. **社会因素**：人们对隐私的担忧阻止了完全自动化的身份系统
2. **经济因素**：柔性显示器等技术存在但成本太高
3. **路径依赖**：我们走向了手机中心化，而非环境分布式的计算模型

这篇文章提醒我们：技术预测往往忽视了社会、经济和政治因素，而这些因素往往比技术可行性更能决定哪些未来会实现。
