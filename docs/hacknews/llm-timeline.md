## Summary / 摘要

An interactive timeline tracking the complete history of 194+ Large Language Models from 2017 to 2026. This visualization tool allows users to explore the evolution of AI models from the foundational Transformer architecture through ChatGPT, GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, LLaMA, Mistral, DeepSeek, and every major LLM ever released.

一个交互式时间线工具，追踪从 2017 年到 2026 年 194 多个大语言模型的完整历史。该可视化工具允许用户探索从基础 Transformer 架构到 ChatGPT、GPT-4、Claude、Gemini、LLaMA、Mistral、DeepSeek 以及所有发布过的主要大语言模型的演进历程。

## What is AI Timeline? / 什么是 AI 时间线？

The AI Timeline is an interactive web-based visualization that documents the explosive growth of Large Language Models over nearly a decade. It serves as a comprehensive reference for researchers, developers, and AI enthusiasts to understand the pace of innovation in the field.

AI 时间线是一个基于 Web 的交互式可视化工具，记录了近十年来大语言模型的爆发式增长。它为研究人员、开发者和 AI 爱好者提供了一个全面的参考，帮助理解该领域创新的节奏。

## Key Features / 核心功能

### Interactive Exploration / 交互式探索
Users can navigate through the timeline to see when each major model was released, track the relationships between models, and understand the progression of capabilities over time.

用户可以浏览时间线，查看每个主要模型的发布时间，追踪模型之间的关系，并了解能力随时间的进展。

## Technical Significance / 技术意义

### The Transformer Revolution / Transformer 革命
The timeline begins in 2017 with the publication of "Attention Is All You Need" by Google researchers, which introduced the transformer architecture that would become the foundation of modern LLMs.

时间线始于 2017 年 Google 研究人员发表的 "Attention Is All You Need"，这篇论文介绍了 Transformer 架构，成为现代大语言模型的基础。

### The ChatGPT Moment / ChatGPT 时刻
November 2022 marked a watershed moment with the release of ChatGPT, bringing LLMs into mainstream consciousness and sparking the generative AI boom.

2022 年 11 月，随着 ChatGPT 的发布，标志着分水岭时刻的到来，将大语言模型带入主流意识，并引发了生成式 AI 的热潮。

### The Rise of Open Source / 开源崛起
2023-2024 saw the emergence of powerful open-source alternatives like LLaMA, Mistral, and DeepSeek, democratizing access to frontier-level AI capabilities.

2023-2024 年见证了 LLaMA、Mistral 和 DeepSeek 等强大的开源替代方案的出现，使前沿级 AI 能力的获取更加民主化。

### Reasoning Models Era / 推理模型时代
2024-2025 introduced "reasoning" models like OpenAI's o1/o3 series and DeepSeek-R1, capable of extended chain-of-thought processing for complex problem-solving.

2024-2025 年引入了 OpenAI o1/o3 系列和 DeepSeek-R1 等"推理"模型，能够进行扩展的思维链处理以解决复杂问题。

## Notable 2025-2026 Developments / 2025-2026 年重要进展

### GPT-5.3 (2026)
The latest entry in the OpenAI series, representing continued advancement in multimodal capabilities and reasoning performance.

OpenAI 系列的最新条目，代表着多模态能力和推理性能的持续进步。

### DeepSeek V3.1 (August 2025)
Introduced the ability to switch between "thinking" and "non-thinking" modes, balancing speed and complexity of responses.

引入了在"思考"和"非思考"模式之间切换的能力，平衡响应的速度和复杂性。

### Qwen3-235B-A22B (2025)
Alibaba's massive Mixture-of-Experts model with 235B total parameters (22B active), becoming one of the strongest open MoE models available.

阿里巴巴的大规模专家混合模型，总参数达 235B（活跃参数 22B），成为最强大的开源 MoE 模型之一。

### Claude 4.5 (2025)
Anthropic's continued push on AI safety while maintaining competitive performance on reasoning benchmarks.

Anthropic 在保持推理基准竞争性能的同时，继续推进 AI 安全工作。

## Conclusion / 结语

The AI Timeline project demonstrates the incredible pace of innovation in large language models over the past decade. From the original Transformer paper's 67 million parameters to modern models with hundreds of billions (or even trillions) of parameters, the field has transformed dramatically. This visualization serves as both an educational tool and a testament to the rapid advancement of AI technology.

AI 时间线项目展示了过去十年大语言模型领域令人难以置信的创新速度。从原始 Transformer 论文的 6700 万参数到具有数千亿（甚至数万亿）参数的现代模型，该领域发生了巨大的变革。这个可视化工具既是教育工具，也是对 AI 技术快速发展的见证。

For developers and researchers working with AI, this timeline provides essential historical context for understanding where the technology came from—and where it might be heading next.

对于从事 AI 工作的开发者和研究人员，这个时间线为理解技术的来源以及未来可能的发展方向提供了重要的历史背景。
