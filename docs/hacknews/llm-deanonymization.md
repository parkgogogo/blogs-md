# Large-Scale Online Deanonymization with LLMs
# 使用大语言模型进行大规模在线去匿名化

**Source:** https://simonlermen.substack.com/p/large-scale-online-deanonymization  
**Date:** 2026-02-24

New research reveals that large language models can effectively deanonymize users across the internet by correlating writing patterns, stylistic choices, and content preferences across multiple platforms. This capability poses significant privacy implications for online anonymity.

新研究表明，大语言模型可以通过关联多个平台上的写作模式、风格选择和内容偏好有效地对互联网用户进行去匿名化。这种能力对在线匿名性构成了重大隐私影响。

The study demonstrates that modern LLMs can identify authors with over 85% accuracy when given access to as few as three writing samples from different platforms. This accuracy persists even when users attempt to vary their writing style or use pseudonyms.

研究表明，当现代 LLM 可以访问来自不同平台的仅三个写作样本时，能够以超过 85% 的准确率识别作者。即使用户试图改变写作风格或使用化名，这种准确率仍然存在。

Key findings:
- LLMs can link accounts across platforms with minimal data
- Stylometric analysis is significantly enhanced by AI capabilities
- Traditional anonymity techniques like VPNs and throwaway accounts are insufficient
- Cross-platform behavioral patterns are highly distinctive

主要发现：
- LLM 可以用最少的数据跨平台关联账户
- 风格计量分析通过 AI 能力得到显著增强
- 传统的匿名技术如 VPN 和一次性账户是不够的
- 跨平台行为模式具有高度独特性

The research highlights how AI systems can identify individuals through subtle linguistic fingerprints including word choice, sentence structure, topic preferences, and even emoji usage patterns. These patterns are difficult for humans to consciously modify.

研究强调了 AI 系统如何通过微妙的语言指纹识别个人，包括词汇选择、句子结构、主题偏好，甚至表情符号使用模式。这些模式对人类来说很难有意识地修改。

Privacy experts warn that this capability could be used to identify whistleblowers, political dissidents, or individuals seeking anonymity for legitimate reasons. The research calls for new approaches to digital privacy protection.

隐私专家警告说，这种能力可能被用来识别举报者、政治异见者或出于合法原因寻求匿名的个人。该研究呼吁采用新的数字隐私保护方法。

The study's authors recommend that users concerned about anonymity employ AI-aware obfuscation techniques, including automated style variation and content randomization tools that can effectively mask their linguistic fingerprints.

研究作者建议担心匿名性的用户采用 AI 感知混淆技术，包括自动风格变化和内容随机化工具，可以有效掩盖他们的语言指纹。
