Title: 神圣之血，诅咒之血 / Blood of the Sacred, Blood of the Damned
URL: https://www.filfre.net/2026/02/gabriel-knight-3-blood-of-the-sacred-blood-of-the-damned/

This article tells part of the story of Jane Jensen.
本文讲述了简·詹森的故事的一部分。

"I think I was convinced in January 1997, when I went to CES and walked around the show and looked at all these hot new titles, and none of the screens had full-motion video. I realized that if anybody was going to look at this game, it was going to have to be 3D."
— Jane Jensen
"我想我是在1997年1月去CES的时候被说服的，我在展会上走来走去，看着所有这些热门新作，却没有一个屏幕上有全动态影像。我意识到，如果想让任何人看这款游戏，它必须是3D的。"——简·詹森

Gabriel Knight 3: Blood of the Sacred, Blood of the Damned proves that miracles do sometimes happen in the games industry. That this game was able to see the light of day at all is miracle enough, seeing as it was made in the teeth of a howling gale directed against adventure games. But the truly magical thing is that the finished product turned out as good as it is.
《狩魔猎人3：神圣之血，诅咒之血》证明了游戏界确实会发生奇迹。这款游戏居然能够问世，本身就已经足够了不起，因为它是顶着针对冒险游戏的狂风巨浪做出来的。但真正神奇的是，它的成品质量竟然如此之好。

In my last article, I told the story of Ultima IX, the tragic end to another iconic series. The tale of Gabriel Knight 3's development is eerily similar to it in broad outline: the same real or perceived need to chase market trends, the same unsupportive management, the same morale problems that led to absurdly high turnover. But Gabriel Knight 3 had one thing that Ultima IX did not. Whereas Richard Garriott, the father of Ultima, always seemed to be elsewhere when there was dirty work to be done, Jane Jensen was on-site for the entirety of her project.
在我的上一篇文章中，我讲述了《创世纪IX》，另一个标志性系列的悲惨结局。《狩魔猎人3》的开发故事在大体轮廓上与它惊人地相似：同样真实的或被认为需要追逐市场趋势的需求、同样不给予支持的管理层、同样导致团队人员流失率高得离谱的士气问题。但《狩魔猎人3》有一点是《创世纪IX》所没有的。如果说《创世纪》之父理查德·加里奥特似乎总是在有人可能要请他亲自动手的时候出现在别处，那么简·詹森则是从头到尾都守在她的项目现场。

Like the first two games, Gabriel Knight 3 ultimately managed to reflect more of her unique vision than some corporate committee's notion of what an adventure game ought to look like in 1999. And that made all the difference.
和前两部游戏一样，《狩魔猎人3》最终设法更多地反映了她的独特愿景，而不是某个公司委员会对1999年冒险游戏应该是什么样子的看法。而这正是关键所在。

In fact, I'm going to go out on a limb right now and drop the bombshell early: despite running contrary to critical consensus, Gabriel Knight 3 is actually my favorite of the trilogy. As always, I don't necessarily expect you to agree with me, but I will do my best to explain just what it is about this game that pleases, fascinates, and even moves me so.
事实上，我现在就要冒险在这里提前抛出本文的重磅炸弹：尽管与批评界的共识相悖，《狩魔猎人3》实际上是三部曲中我最喜欢的一部。和往常一样，我不一定期望你同意我的观点，但我会尽力解释这款游戏究竟在哪些方面让我如此愉悦、着迷，甚至感动。

But before we get to that, we need to turn the knob on our time machine back a few years from 1999, to late 1995, when Jane Jensen had just finished her second Gabriel Knight game, The Beast Within. That game was born of a brief, manic era at Sierra On-Line when the company's founders, Ken and Roberta Williams, were convinced that the necessary future of mass-market games was a collision of Silicon Valley and Hollywood: players would be making decisions for real actors they saw on the screen.
不过，在我们谈到这些之前，我们需要将时间机器的旋钮从1999年再往回拨几年，拨到1995年末，那时简·詹森刚刚完成她的第二部《狩魔猎人》游戏《野兽之心》。那款游戏诞生于雪乐山在线一个短暂而狂热的时代，当时公司的创始人肯和罗伯塔·威廉姆斯坚信，大众市场游戏的必要未来是硅谷与好莱坞的思想碰撞：玩家将为屏幕上看到的真实演员做出决策。

Sierra was so committed to this future that it built its own professional-grade recording studio in its hometown of Oakhurst, California. Gabriel Knight 2 was the second game to emerge from that facility, following Roberta Williams's million-selling Phantasmagoria. Yet for all that Gabriel Knight 2 was far better as both a game and a work of fiction than that lowbrow schlock-fest, it sold only a fraction as many copies.
雪乐山对这一未来如此投入，以至于它在加州小城奥克赫斯特的家乡建造了自己的专业级录音棚。《狩魔猎人2》是该设施产出的第二款游戏，继罗伯塔·威廉姆斯销量百万的《幻影》之后。然而，尽管《狩魔猎人2》作为一款游戏和一部小说作品，表现得远比那部低俗的血浆片要好得多，但它的销量却只有后者的一小部分。

"I thought we did a really great job," Jensen said. "I thought it would appeal to that mass market. I thought it would be in the top ten. It was — for about a week. I looked at the charts for months after it came out, looking at the games that outsold it, and I thought, 'You know, I'm in the wrong business.'"
"我觉得我们做得非常出色，"詹森说，"我觉得它会吸引那个大众市场。我觉得它会进入前十名。它确实进了——大约一个星期。我在发行后的几个月里看着排行榜，看着那些销量超过它的游戏，我想，'你知道吗，我入错行了。'"

These disappointing sales figures affected more than just Jane Jensen's psyche. Combined with similarly disappointing sales figures from other like-minded games, they derailed the "Silicon-Hollywood" train almost before it left the station. In the aftermath, everyone was left to ponder sobering questions about the fate of the Gabriel Knight series, about the fate of Sierra, and about the fate of adventure games in general.
这些令人失望的 sales figures 影响的不仅仅是简·詹森的心理。加上其他类似游戏同样令人失望的 sales figures，它们让"硅谷-好莱坞"列车在出站不久就脱轨了。在 aftermath 中，每个人都被留下来思考关于《狩魔猎人》系列命运、关于雪乐山命运、以及关于冒险游戏整体命运的严峻问题。

The proposal to make a third Gabriel Knight game did not come immediately. Jane Jensen took a year-long sabbatical from Sierra, busying herself with writing novelizations of the first two games for Roc Books. During her absence, new, more action-oriented genres — first-person shooters and real-time strategy — completed their conquest of the mainstream of computer gaming, while Sierra itself was acquired by an unlikely buyer whose sources and intentions were unclear: CUC.
制作第三部《狩魔猎人》游戏的提议并没有立即出现。简·詹森从雪乐山休了一年的假，忙于为Roc Books撰写前两部游戏的小说化版本。在她离开期间，第一人称射击和即时战略这些新的、更注重动作的 genre 完成了对电脑游戏主流市场的征服，而雪乐山本身也被一个来源和意图都不明确的 unlikely buyer——CUC——收购了。

Thus when she returned to Sierra, she found everything different, her bubbling-over excitement about a new idea in tow. During her time away, she had read a supposedly non-fiction book called The Tomb of God, the latest entry in the vast, tangled literature about the French village of Rennes-le-Château.
因此，当她回到雪乐山时，她发现一切都不同了，她带着一个新想法的兴奋之情 bubbling over。在休息期间，她读了一本据称是非虚构的书《上帝的坟墓》，这是关于法国小村庄雷恩-勒-夏托的大量错综复杂文献中的最新一部。

These stories begin with a mysteriously wealthy nineteenth-century priest and rumors of certain treasures he may have hidden in and around the village, then swell in the telling to incorporate the Holy Grail, Mary Magdalene, the Knights Templar, the Freemasons, the true bloodline of Jesus Christ, and the inevitable millennial conspiracy to control the world and hide the truth. This bizarre cottage industry would reach its commercial apotheosis in the early 2000s with Dan Brown's novel The Da Vinci Code and its film adaptation.
这些故事始于一位神秘富有的19世纪牧师以及关于他可能在村庄内外隐藏的某些宝藏的传言，然后在讲述中不断膨胀，纳入了圣杯、抹大拉的马利亚、圣殿骑士团、共济会、耶稣基督的真正血脉，以及控制世界和隐藏真相的 inevitable 千年阴谋。这个 bizarre 的家庭手工业将在21世纪初达到商业巅峰，以丹·布朗的小说《达·芬奇密码》及其改编电影为代表。

It isn't clear whether Jensen herself actually believed any of these claims, but she did see a way to add vampires to the mix — she had always intended for the third Gabriel Knight game to involve vampires — and make it into a game that wove history and horror together, just as Gabriel Knight 2 had so boldly proposed that "Mad King" Ludwig II was a werewolf and then proceeded to make an incredibly cogent case for that crazy assertion.
目前尚不清楚詹森本人是否真的相信这些说法中的任何一个，但她确实看到了一种将吸血鬼加入其中的方法——她一直打算让第三部《狩魔猎人》游戏涉及吸血鬼——并将其变成一款将历史和恐怖融合在一起的游戏，就像《狩魔猎人2》那样大胆地提出"疯王"路德维希二世是狼人的假设，然后继续为这个疯狂的主张提出令人难以置信的合理 case。

Sierra's new management agreed to make the game, for reasons that are not crystal clear but can perhaps be inferred. It was late 1996, still early enough that a sufficiently determined industry observer could argue that the adventure game's recent failure to generate any new million-selling hits was more a fluke than a long-term trend. Ken Williams was still present at Sierra, albeit with much-diminished influence compared to the days when he called all the shots. For better or for worse, he had always liked the idea of "controversial" games. The future Gabriel Knight 3 looked certain to fit that bill, being based on a heretical premise: that Jesus Christ was not celibate, that in fact he was married to Mary Magdalene and had children with her in the biological, less-than-holy way.
雪乐山的新管理层同意制作这款游戏，原因并不 crystal clear，但或许可以推断。那是1996年末，仍然足够早，以至于一个足够坚定的 industry observer 可以辩称，冒险游戏 genre 近期未能产生任何新的百万销量热门作品，这可能更多是一个偶然事件而非长期趋势。肯·威廉姆斯仍然出现在雪乐山的现场，尽管与他独自发号施令的年代相比，他的影响力已大大减弱。无论好坏，他一直喜欢"有争议"游戏的想法。未来的《狩魔猎人3》看起来肯定符合这一 bill，因为它基于一个异端的前提：耶稣基督并非独身，事实上他与抹大拉的马利亚结婚，并以生物学的、不那么圣洁的方式与她生育了孩子。

Centuries ago, saying such things would get you drawn and quartered or burnt at the stake; now, it would merely outrage every priest, preacher, and parishioner within earshot. It was a way to get people talking about adventure games again.
几个世纪前，说这种话会让你被 drawn and quartered 或 burnt at the stake；现在，它只会让每个牧师、传教士和会众成员气得说不出话来。这是让人们再次谈论冒险游戏的一种方式。

Even so, things could not remain the same for the genre. Times were changing: digitized human actors were out, real-time 3D was in, and even such an anachronistic holdover as this one would have to adapt. Thus Gabriel Knight 3 would be done in immersive 3D, both because it offered more flexibility than the static photographs and chunks of pre-rendered video that Gabriel Knight 2 had revolved around, and because it was theoretically at least much cheaper than trying to film a whole troupe of professional actors cavorting in a recording studio. This new game would be made at Sierra's new offices in Bellevue, Washington, where the company had gradually been moving development over the last few years.
即便如此，对于这个 genre 来说，一切也不可能照旧。时代在变：数字化的人类演员已经过时，实时3D才是王道，即使是这样一个不合时宜的落伍 genre 也必须适应。因此，《狩魔猎人3》将以沉浸式3D完成，这既是因为与《狩魔猎人2》所围绕的静态照片和成段预制视频相比，它提供了更大的灵活性，也是因为理论上至少，它比试图拍摄一整群专业演员在录音棚里嬉戏要便宜得多。这款新游戏将在雪乐山位于华盛顿州贝尔维尤的新办公室制作，公司在过去几年里已经逐渐将开发工作转移到那里。

Jane Jensen officially returned to Sierra in December 1996 to begin writing the script and design document, even as a group of engineers started work on the core technology. The planned ship date was Christmas 1998. But from the start, there were aspects of the project that called that timeline into question.
简·詹森于1996年12月正式回到雪乐山，开始编写剧本和设计文档，同时一组工程师开始着手核心技术的开发。计划的 ship date 是1998年圣诞节。但从一开始，就有一些项目方面让人质疑这个时间线的可行性。

Sierra effectively had three projects, all attempting to update the company's older adventure franchises for the new era of real-time 3D. Yet there was no attempt to develop a single shared engine to drive them, despite the example of SCI — one of the key building blocks of Sierra's early success, which had driven all of its 2D adventure games from late 1988 onward — being right there in front of them. Gabriel Knight 3 was the last of the three 3D projects to get started, well behind King's Quest: Mask of Eternity and Quest for Glory V. Its engine, known as the G-Engine for obvious reasons, was largely the creation of a software engineer named Jim Napier, who laid its foundations in the first half of 1997.
雪乐山实际上同时有三个项目，都在试图为实时3D的新时代更新公司较老的冒险系列。然而，却没有尝试开发一个单一的共享引擎来驱动它们，尽管SCI的例子就在眼前——它是雪乐山早期成功的关键 building blocks 之一，从1988年末开始就驱动了它的所有2D冒险游戏。《狩魔猎人3》是三个3D项目中最后启动的，远在《国王密使：永恒面具》和《 Quest for Glory V》之后。它的引擎，出于明显的原因被称为G引擎，主要是一位名叫吉姆·内皮尔的软件工程师的创作，他在1997年上半年奠定了它的基础。

Unfortunately, Napier was moved over to work on SWAT 3 after that, leaving the tech stack in less than ideal shape. This sudden reassignment would become a recurring theme. The people who worked on Gabriel Knight 3 were treated as the dregs of the employee roster, sentenced to toil on Sierra's least commercially promising game. No wonder poor morale and high turnover would be constant issues for the project.
不幸的是，在那之后内皮尔被调去参与《SWAT 3》的工作，让技术栈处于 less than ideal 的状态。这种突然的调动将成为一个反复出现的主题。参与《狩魔猎人3》工作的人被当作员工名册上的 dregs，被判处为雪乐山商业前景最黯淡的游戏辛苦劳作。难怪 poor morale 和高人员流失率将成为该项目的 constant issues。

Close to fifty people would be assigned to Gabriel Knight 3 before it was all over, but never more than twenty at any one time. These included two producers, three art directors, and three project leads. The continuing chaos, combined with the determination to reinvent the 3D-adventure wheel anew at every turn, undermined any and all cost savings that the switch from digitized video to 3D graphics ought to have brought. Originally projected to cost about $1.5 million, Gabriel Knight 3 wound up costing no less than $4.2 million to complete. It was never canceled, more due to inertia and the equally manic churn rate in Sierra's executive suites than any real belief in the game's potential.
在一切结束之前，将近50人将被分配到《狩魔猎人3》，但同一时间从未超过20人。其中包括两位制作人、三位艺术总监和三位项目负责人。持续的混乱，加上每次转动时都要重新发明3D冒险车轮的决心，破坏了从数字化视频转向3D图形本应带来的任何和所有成本节约。最初预计成本约为150万美元，《狩魔猎人3》最终完成时的成本不低于420万美元。它从未被取消，更多是由于惯性以及雪乐山高管套房中同样疯狂的 churn rate，而不是对游戏潜力的任何真正 belief。

For her part, Jane Jensen displayed amazing resilience and professionalism throughout the process. She had rushed on Gabriel Knight 2, submitting a script that had to be cut by 25 percent or more during development, leaving plot holes and gameplay gaps that had to be imperfectly papered over. This time, she kept in mind that game design, like politics, is the art of the possible. For all the problems, remarkably little of her design had to be cut.
就她而言，简·詹森在整个过程中表现出惊人的韧性和专业精神。她在《狩魔猎人2》上操之过急，提交的剧本在开发过程中不得不削减25%或更多，留下了一些丑陋的情节和游戏漏洞，只能 imperfectly paper over。这一次，她牢记游戏开发就像政治一样，是可能性的艺术。尽管有所有问题，她的设计中很少有被削减的。

The people around her were a mix of fresh faces who were there because they had been ordered to be, and old-timers who shared her passion for this set of themes and characters. Among the latter were her husband Robert Holmes, who provided a third brooding yet hummable score for the series, and Stuart Rosen, who had directed the voice-actor cast for Gabriel Knight 1.
她周围的人是一群新面孔的混合体，他们之所以在那里是因为被命令去的，还有一些 old-timers，他们分享她对这组主题和角色的热情。在这些 latter 中包括她的丈夫罗伯特·福尔摩斯，他为该系列提供了第三部忧郁 yet hummable 的配乐，以及斯图·罗森，他曾执导《狩魔猎人1》的配音演员阵容。

Rosen convinced Tim Curry to return — he had voiced the protagonist in the first game, but had been absent from the live-action Gabriel Knight 2. His hammy New Orleans accent was not to everyone's taste, but it did provide a welcome note of continuity through all the technical changes the series had undergone. Recording sessions began as early as November 1997, right after Jane Jensen returned from her first in-person visit to Rennes-le-Château.
罗森说服了蒂姆·库里回归——他曾为第一部游戏中的主角配音，但在真人出演的《狩魔猎人2》中缺席。他夸张的新奥尔良口音并非人人喜欢，但它确实为系列经历的所有技术变化提供了一种 welcome 的连续性音符。录音会话早在1997年11月就开始了，就在简·詹森从她对雷恩-勒-夏托的第一次亲自访问回来之后。

But as we saw with Ultima IX, such sessions are only superficial markers of progress, and thus often the refuge of those in denial about more fundamental problems. When an engineer named Scott Bilas arrived in early 1998 to become Gabriel Knight 3's latest technical lead, he concluded that "the engineering team must have been living in some kind of magical dream world. I can't explain it any other way. At that point in time, the game was a hacked-up version of the sample application Jim Napier had written earlier to demo the G-Engine."
但正如我们在《创世纪IX》中看到的那样，这样的会话只是 progress 的 superficial 标志，因此常常是那些否认更根本问题的人的避难所。当一位名叫斯科特·比拉斯的工程师于1998年初到来，成为《狩魔猎人3》最新的技术负责人时，他得出结论："工程团队一定一直生活在一个神奇的梦境世界里。我找不到其他方式来解释。在那个时候，游戏是吉姆·内皮尔早些时候为演示G引擎而编写的示例应用程序的一个 hacked-up 版本。"

Bilas spent months restructuring the G-Engine and adding a SCI-like scripting language called Sheep to separate game design from underlying engine programming. His postmortem of the project for Game Developer magazine, written about six months after Gabriel Knight 3's release, makes for brutal reading.
比拉斯花了几个月的时间重新设计G引擎，并添加了一种类似SCI的脚本语言Sheep，以将游戏设计与底层引擎编程分离。他为《游戏开发者》杂志撰写的项目 postmortem，大约在《狩魔猎人3》发行六个月后，读起来令人 brutal。

For most of the people assigned to this project, he wrote, it was more death march than labor of love, a veritable encyclopedia of worst practices in project management. Throughout the project, (love and appreciation from Sierra management) was severely lacking. Recognition for work done (other than relief when it was finished) was very rare, insincere when given, and always too little too late. Internally, many on the team thought the game was of poor quality.
对于被分配到这个项目的大多数人来说，它更像是一场死亡行军，而不是一项 labor of love，是项目管理 worst practices 的一本 veritable 百科全书。在整个项目中，（来自雪乐山管理层的）爱和欣赏严重缺乏。对工作的认可（除了完成时的 relief）非常罕见，缺乏诚意，而且总是太少、太晚。在内部，团队中的很多人认为游戏质量很差。

Certainly, many websites and magazines proclaiming that "adventure games are dead" only made things worse. Tim Schafer's Grim Fandango, while a brilliant game and widely acclaimed, was supposedly (we heard) not doing well in the marketplace...
当然，许多网站和杂志宣称"冒险游戏已死"只会让情况变得更糟。蒂姆·谢弗的《冥界狂想曲》，虽然是一款出色的游戏且广受好评，但据说（我们听说）在市场上表现不佳……

Low morale resulted in many going-away lunches for developers seeking greener pastures. Gabriel Knight 3 had ridiculous turnover; unnecessary if these people had been properly assigned or treated well... After working on a project like this for a certain amount of time, morale can sink so low that the team develops incredible passive resistance to any kind of change. Developers can become so burned out on a project, and build up so much hatred for it, that they avoid doing anything that might delay the ship date. This was a terrible problem during the second half of the development cycle for Gabriel Knight 3...
低士气导致了很多为寻求 greener pastures 的开发者举办的送行午餐。《狩魔猎人3》有 ridiculous 的人员流失率，如果这些人被正确分配或得到良好对待，这本是不必要的……在一个像这样的项目上工作了一定时间后，士气可能会降到如此之低，以至于团队对任何 kind of change 都发展出难以置信的被动抵抗。开发者可能会对项目感到如此疲惫，并对它建立如此多的仇恨，以至于他们避免做任何可能让 ship date 推迟的事情。这是在《狩魔猎人3》开发周期的后半段的一个 terrible problem……

Our engineers never had an accurate development schedule; what we had were schedules that were obviously wrong to the point where everyone on the team knew there was no way to meet them. Our leads often lied to management about progress, tasks, and estimates, I believe because they were over their heads and didn't react well to the pressure. So upper management thought the project would stabilize and be ready to ship much earlier than it really was, and we faced long crunch times to deliver promised features...
我们的工程师从来没有一个准确的开发进度表；我们有的进度表明显是错误的，以至于团队中的每个人都知道没有办法 meet them。我们的负责人经常对管理层撒谎，关于进度、任务和估计，我相信这是因为他们已经 over their heads，并且对压力反应不佳。因此，上层管理层认为项目会比实际上更早稳定并准备好 ship，我们面临长时间的 crunch times 来交付承诺的功能……

Much of the last year of the project was spent in [crunch] mode, which means that even small breaks like vacations, conference attendance, and often even evenings and weekends off were frowned upon. This was "time the team couldn't afford to lose." Ironically, this overtime didn't help anyway; the project didn't move any faster or release any earlier. The lack of respect for personal lives and concern for our well-being caused our morale to drop...
项目最后一年的大部分时间我们都是在[crunch]模式下度过的，这意味着即使是度假、参加会议的小休息，甚至经常连晚上和周末休息都被看不起。这是团队"不能失去的时间"。具有讽刺意味的是，这种加班无论如何都没有帮助；项目并没有移动得更快或更早 release。对个人生活缺乏尊重和对我们 well-being 的关注导致我们的士气下降……

Gabriel Knight 3 became a black hole that sucked in many developers from other projects, often at the expense of those projects. Artists were moved off the team to lower the burn rate, then pulled back later because there was still so much work to do...
《狩魔猎人3》成了一个黑洞，吞噬了许多来自其他项目的开发者，经常以牺牲这些项目为代价。艺术家被调离团队以降低 burn rate，然后在后来又被拉回来，因为还有如此多的工作要做……

Management, thinking this would save time, often encouraged content developers to hack and work around issues rather than properly fixing them...
管理层认为这样可以节省时间，经常鼓励内容开发者 hack 和 work around 问题，而不是正确地修复它们……

All of this was happening against a backdrop of thoroughgoing chaos and dysfunction at Sierra in general. The marginalized Ken Williams had had enough, and left the company he had founded in August 1997. At the end of that year, Sierra's new parent company CUC merged with another large conglomerate, HFS, to create a new entity called Cendant. Just a few months later, CUC was revealed to have been a house of cards all along, the locus of one of the biggest accounting scandals in American business history.
所有这一切都发生在雪乐山整体 thoroughgoing 混乱和功能障碍的背景下。被边缘化的肯·威廉姆斯受够了，于1997年8月离开了他创立的公司。那年年底，雪乐山的新母公司CUC与另一家大型 conglomerate HFS合并，创建了一个名为Cendant的新实体。仅仅几个月后，CUC被 reveal 一直是纸牌屋，是美国商业史上最大的会计丑闻之一的 locus。

For much of Gabriel Knight 3's production, there was reason to doubt whether there would even be a Sierra for the team to report to in a week or a month's time. Finally, in November 1998, Sierra was acquired yet again, this time by the French media giant Vivendi, whose long-term plan, slowly becoming apparent, was to terminate all internal game development and use the brand's name recognition to turn it into a pure publisher. Needless to say, this didn't do much for the morale of the people still there making games.
在《狩魔猎人3》制作的很长一段时间里，有理由怀疑一周或一个月后是否还会有雪乐山让团队去报告。最后，在1998年11月，雪乐山再次被收购，这次是法国媒体巨头维旺迪，其长期计划，慢慢地变得明显，是终止所有内部游戏开发，利用该品牌的知名度将其转变为纯粹的发行商。不用说，这对仍然在那里制作游戏的人的士气毫无帮助。

Sierra's Oakhurst offices were closed in February 1999. The first wave of layoffs swept through Bellevue the following summer, even as the Gabriel Knight 3 team was desperately trying to get the game out for Christmas 1999 rather than 1998. In a stunning proof of corporate cluelessness about human psychology, some of the people working on Gabriel Knight 3 were straightforwardly told that they were going to be fired, but not before they gave their last drop of blood, sweat, and tears to finish the game.
雪乐山的奥克赫斯特办事处于1999年2月关闭。第一波裁员在次年夏天席卷贝尔维尤，而《狩魔猎人3》团队正拼命努力让游戏在1999年圣诞节而不是1998年上市。在对人类心理的 corporate cluelessness 的一个惊人证明中，一些参与《狩魔猎人3》工作的人被直截了当地告知他们将被解雇，但不是在他们为完成游戏付出最后一滴血、汗和泪之前。

"Having a group of (understandably) disgruntled people who are upset about being fired by your company and are actively looking for work, while still trying to contribute to a project, is a delicate situation that should be avoided," Scott Bilas said mildly. "No shit, Sherlock" would seem to apply here.
"让一群（可以理解地）对你们公司解雇他们感到不满并积极找工作的人，同时仍然试图为项目做出贡献，这是一个应该避免的敏感情况，"斯科特·比拉斯轻描淡写地说。"废话，福尔摩斯"这样的话似乎在这里适用。

But there was one person who received sustained praise in Bilas's postmortem: Jane Jensen, whose vision and commitment never wavered. "If we had a less experienced designer, Gabriel Knight 3 would have just fallen over and died. Throughout development, the one thing we could count on was the game design. It was well thought-out and researched, with an interesting and compelling story. Best of all, Jane got it right early; other than some of the puzzles, nothing really needed to be redesigned during development. She delivered the design on time and maintained it meticulously throughout the project."
但在比拉斯的 postmortem 中，有一个人受到了持续的赞扬，那就是简·詹森，她的愿景和承诺从未动摇。"如果我们有一位经验不足的设计师，《狩魔猎人3》就会直接倒下并死去。在整个开发过程中，我们可以指望的一件事就是游戏设计。它经过深思熟虑和研究，有一个有趣且引人入胜的故事。最棒的是，简很早就把它做对了；除了一些谜题，没有什么真的需要在开发过程中重新设计。她按时交付了设计，并在项目进行过程中一丝不苟地维护它。"

This was Gabriel Knight 3's secret weapon, the reason it didn't turn into a disaster like Ultima IX. With Jane Jensen on board, there was always someone to turn to who knew what the game was supposed to do and be. Vision matters.
这是《狩魔猎人3》的秘密武器，是它没有变成像《创世纪IX》那样的灾难的原因。有简·詹森在船上，总有人可以求助，他知道游戏应该做什么和成为什么。愿景这东西很重要。

When Gabriel Knight 3: Blood of the Sacred, Blood of the Damned was released in November 1999, it marked the definitive end of an era. It was Sierra's last adventure game, the final destination of a cultural tradition stretching back nearly two decades to Mystery House, back to a time when the computer-games industry was more inchoate than concrete.
当《狩魔猎人3：神圣之血，诅咒之血》于1999年11月发行时，它标志着一个时代的 definitive 终结，是雪乐山最后一部冒险游戏，是文化传统的 final destination，这一传统可以追溯到近二十年前的《神秘屋》，回到电脑游戏产业比 concrete 更 inchoate 的时代。

In the early 1990s, the commercial heyday of the graphic adventure, Sierra and LucasArts had been the yin and yang of the space, the bones of endless partisan contentions among players. Thus it is intriguing and perhaps instructive to compare the media reception of Gabriel Knight 3 to that of LucasArts's latest high-profile adventure game of 1998, Grim Fandango.
在图形冒险游戏商业巅峰的1990年代初，雪乐山和LucasArts一直是该领域的阴阳两极，是玩家间 endless partisan contentions 的骨头。因此，将《狩魔猎人3》的媒体 reception 与1998年LucasArts最新一部高调冒险游戏《冥界狂想曲》的 reception 进行比较，是 intriguing 且 perhaps instructive 的。

Grim Fandango was treated by reviewers as something of a cause célèbre, who rightly praised its unusual setting, vividly drawn characters, and moving story, even as they paid less attention to its clumsy interface and complex, illogical puzzle structures. The people who wrote about games tended to be a few years older on average than the people who simply played them. Many in this generation of journalists had grown up with Maniac Mansion and/or The Secret of Monkey Island. They were haunted by the idea of a LucasArts that no longer made adventure games, and tried to make this one successful enough to prevent that outcome.
《冥界狂想曲》被评论家们当作某种 cause célèbre，他们正确地赞扬了其不寻常的设定、生动描绘的角色和感人的故事，即使他们对它笨拙的界面和复杂且不合逻辑的谜题结构关注较少。写游戏的人往往比单纯玩游戏的人平均大几岁。这一代记者中的许多人是伴随着《疯狂豪宅》和/或《猴岛的秘密》长大的。他们被一个不再制作冒险游戏的LucasArts的想法所困扰，并试图让这款游戏足够成功以避免那种结果。

Sometimes their reviews almost took on a hectoring tone: you simply must buy this game, they lectured their readers. This pressure campaign failed to fully accomplish its goal; Grim Fandango was not a complete flop, but it did no better than break even, failing to provide LucasArts with a particularly convincing financial argument to make more such games.
有时，他们的评论几乎带有 hectoring 的语气：你必须买这款游戏，他们向读者 lectured。这场 pressure campaign 未能 fully accomplish its goal；《冥界狂想曲》并非彻底的 flop，但它充其量也只是收支平衡，没有为LucasArts提供特别有说服力的财务论据来制作更多这样的游戏。

Alas, Gabriel Knight 3, which arrived a year later, was not granted the same benefit of the doubt on its strengths and weaknesses as Grim Fandango. Some reviews were not merely negative but savagely so, almost as if the authors were angry at the game for daring to exist in this era. GameSpot declared that this third installment was "for only the most die-hard fans of the series." Even the generally sober-minded Computer Gaming World, the industry's closest thing to a mature record, had its knives out for this game.
唉，一年后 arrive 的《狩魔猎人3》并没有像《冥界狂想曲》那样在优缺点上得到同样的 benefit of the doubt。一些评论不仅仅是负面的，而且是 savagely so，几乎好像作者对游戏在这个时代敢于存在感到愤怒。GameSpot 宣称这第三部"只适合该系列最死忠的粉丝"。即使是 generally sober-minded 的《电脑游戏世界》，该行业最接近成熟 record 的期刊，也对这款游戏 knives out。

In a two-star (out of five) review, Tom Chick said that "you'll spend a lot of time fumbling around in limbo, wandering aimlessly, trying to trigger any unknowable behavior to end the time block." Okay — but this criticism is difficult to square with that same magazine's four-and-a-half-star "Editor's Choice" award-winning review of Grim Fandango. Aimless wandering and unknowable behavior were, at least in my experience, far more prevalent in that game than in Gabriel Knight 3, which does a much better job of telling you what your objectives are from one story beat to the next.
在一份两星（满分五星）的评论中，汤姆·奇克说"你会花很多时间在 limbo 中摸索，漫无目的地游荡，试图触发任何 unknowable 的行为来结束时间段。"好的——但这种批评很难与该杂志给《冥界狂想曲》的四星半"编辑选择"获奖评论相协调。至少在我的经验中，漫无目的的游荡和 unknowable 的行为在那款游戏中比《狩魔猎人3》更为普遍，《狩魔猎人3》在从一个故事节拍告诉你的目标方面做得要好得多。

What might be going on here? First, we must consider that LucasArts historically enjoyed better reviews and more benefit of the doubt than Sierra, whose adventure games came more frequently but did tend in the aggregate to be rougher around the edges. Yet I don't think this fully explains the contrast.
这里可能发生了什么？首先，我们必须考虑到，历史上LucasArts比雪乐山享有更好的评论和更多的 benefit of the doubt，后者的冒险游戏来得更频繁，但 aggregate 上确实 tend to be rougher around the edges。然而，我认为这并不能完全解释这种对比。

In 1998, the taste-making mainstream of gaming was still in the bargaining phase with adventure games, still trying to find a place for them amidst all the changes that id Software had unleashed on the world with DOOM. A year later, that bargaining was being given up as a lost cause. Adventure games, the new conventional wisdom went, were well and truly dead, never to return. A pack mentality kicked in, with everyone rushing to pile on. It is a disconcerting, perhaps even disturbing thing to witness, but sometimes that's what we call human nature.
1998年，主流游戏界的品味制定者在冒险游戏方面仍处于 bargaining phase，仍在试图在id Software向世界释放DOOM以来发生的所有变化中为它们找到一个位置。一年后，这种讨价还价被当作 lost cause 放弃了。冒险游戏，新的 conventional wisdom 说，已经死透了，不会回来了。一种 pack mentality kicked in，每个人都 rush 去 pile on。这是一种 disconcerting，也许 even disturbing 的事情 to witness，但有时这就是我们所称的人性。

If our recent social history has taught us anything, it's that cultural change can burst upon the scene with head-snapping speed and force, leaving yesterday's conventional wisdom suddenly beyond the pale today.
如果我们最近几年的社会历史告诉我们什么，那就是文化变革可以以令人 head-snapping 的速度和力量 burst upon the scene，让昨天的 conventional wisdom 突然在今天变得 beyond the pale。

Adventure games would soon disappear from the catalogs of major publishers, and from the catalogs of the magazines and websites that covered them. In one of the rare sympathetic takes on the genre's travails, the website Gamecenter wrote after Gabriel Knight 3's release and its less than awe-inspiring commercial performance that "it seems people want more action than adventure these days. They'd rather run around in short pants raiding tombs than experience a real story." This is the real heart of the matter, even if the dismissive tone is as unnecessary here as when it's directed in the opposite direction.
冒险游戏很快就会从主要发行商的产品目录中消失，也从关注它们的杂志和网站的目录中消失。在罕见的对该 genre travails 的同情 take 中，网站Gamecenter在《狩魔猎人3》发行和其 less than awe-inspiring 商业表现之后写道，"现在似乎人们想要更多的动作而不是冒险。他们宁愿穿着短裤跑来跑去盗墓，也不愿体验真正的故事。"这是问题的真正核心，尽管这种轻蔑的语气在这里和当它被 directed in the opposite direction 时一样不必要。

People simply wanted different things; a player of Gabriel Knight 3 was not necessarily smarter, more intelligent, or more cultured than a player of StarCraft or Unreal Tournament.
人们只是想要不同的东西；一个《狩魔猎人3》的玩家并不一定比一个《星际争霸》或《虚幻竞技场》的玩家更聪明、更有智慧或更有文化修养。

So then, to state the obvious, the core problem for adventure games was the mismatch between what most players wanted at the turn of the millennium and what adventure games could provide. The eventual solution was to let the remaining adventure-game fans have their own cottage industry to make the games they liked for them, plus their own media ecosystem to cover them, staffed by sympathetic critics who wanted the same things from their games as their readers.
那么，冒着陈述 obvious 的风险，冒险游戏的核心问题在于千禧年之际大多数玩家的欲望与冒险游戏能提供的东西之间的不匹配。最终的解决方案是让剩下的冒险游戏粉丝拥有自己的家庭手工业来为他们制作他们喜欢的游戏，加上他们自己的媒体生态系统来覆盖它们，配备有同情心的评论家，他们想要从游戏中得到的东西与他们的读者相同。

The entire corpus of computer games was able to sustain being split into parallel ecosystems, which speaks to how big the tent had become over the course of the 1990s. But as of 1999, that siloing hadn't quite happened, leaving games like Gabriel Knight 3 stranded on a stage in an unfriendly theater, playing to an audience that was no longer interested in the type of entertainment they were peddling.
整个电脑游戏能够 sustain 被分割成平行的生态系统，这证明了1990年代期间帐篷已经变得有多大。但截至1999年，这种 siloing 还没有 quite happen，让像《狩魔猎人3》这样的游戏被困在一个不友好的剧院的舞台上，面对一群不再对它推销的娱乐类型感兴趣的观众。

Jane Jensen had mused about the potentially controversy-generating elements while the game was still in development: "I guess the worst-case scenario is that nobody cares enough to even notice." The worst-case scenario came to pass. Gabriel Knight 3 became the sacrificial lamb of its tragic genre, the only controversy being that it dared to exist as an ambitious adventure game in 1999. It deserved better treatment, for reasons I'm about to explain.
在游戏仍在开发中时，简·詹森曾对那些可能有助于获得资金的争议元素进行过思考："我想最坏的情况是没有人会关心，甚至不会注意到。"最坏的情况成真了。《狩魔猎人3》成了它那个悲惨 genre 的 sacrificial lamb，唯一的争议在于它作为1999年一款雄心勃勃的冒险游戏敢于存在。它值得更好的对待，原因我现在要讲。

This final Sierra adventure opens with something else that wouldn't long survive 1999: a story setup conveyed through a manual — or rather, through a comic book that came packaged with the game — rather than through a cutscene. Four years after hunting werewolves in Gabriel Knight 2, Gabriel and his assistant Grace Nakamura are invited to the Paris mansion of one Prince James, a descendant of the House of Stuart that once ruled Scotland and England.
这最后一部雪乐山冒险游戏以一个在1999年同样不会长久存在的东西开场：一个通过手册——或者更确切地说，通过一本随附的漫画书——而不是过场动画传达的故事设定。在《狩魔猎人2》中猎杀狼人四年后，加布里埃尔和他的助手格蕾丝·中村被邀请到一位詹姆斯王子的巴黎豪宅，他是曾经统治苏格兰和英格兰的斯图亚特家族的后裔。

Upon their arrival, the good prince explains that he needs help protecting his infant son from "night visitors" — i.e., vampires. Gabriel and Grace agree to take on the task, only to fail in fairly emphatic fashion; the baby is kidnapped from right under their noses that very night. But Gabriel does manage to give pursuit, tracking the people or creatures who absconded with the infant to the vicinity of Rennes-le-Château. Uncertain how to proceed next, he checks into the village's one hotel. The game proper begins the next morning.
他们到达后，这位好王子解释说，他需要帮助保护他的婴儿儿子免受"夜访者"——即吸血鬼——的伤害。加布里埃尔和格蕾丝同意承担这项任务，却以相当强调的方式失败了；婴儿就在那天晚上从他们鼻子底下被绑架了。但加布里埃尔确实设法追击，追踪那些带着婴儿潜逃的人或怪物到了雷恩-勒-夏托附近。不确定接下来该如何进行，他入住了村里的一家酒店。游戏正式开始于第二天早上。

Over breakfast, he learns that a gaggle of treasure-hunting tourists has also just arrived at the hotel, all dreaming of the fortune reputedly hidden somewhere in or around the village. In addition to the fetching French tour guide Madeleine — to whom Gabriel reacts in his standard lecherous fashion — there is Emilio, a stoic Middle Easterner; Lady Lily Howard and Estelle Stiles, a British noblewoman and her companion; John Wilkes, a strutting, muscle-bound Australian; and Vittorio Buchelli, a prickly Italian scholar.
在早餐时，他了解到一群寻宝游客也刚刚抵达酒店，他们都梦想着据说隐藏在村庄内外某处的财富。除了迷人的法国导游玛德琳（加布里埃尔以他标准的 lecherous 方式对她做出反应）之外，还有埃米利奥，一个坚忍的中东人；莉莉·霍华德夫人和埃斯特尔·斯泰尔斯，一位英国贵族和她的同伴；约翰·威尔克斯，一个傲慢的、肌肉发达的澳洲人；以及维托里奥·布凯利，一个易怒的意大利学者。

In this cast of characters worthy of an Agatha Christie novel, we must add Gabriel's old New Orleans sidekick Detective Frank Mosely, who, in a coincidence that would have Charles Dickens spinning in his grave, just happens to have joined this tour group to try his hand at treasure hunting. Each member of the group has their own theories about the true nature of the treasure and how to find it, leaving Gabriel to try to figure out which are truly hopeless amateurs and which have relevant secrets to hide, possibly involving the kidnapping that brought him here.
在这个值得阿加莎·克里斯蒂小说的角色阵容中，我们必须加上加布里埃尔在新奥尔良的老搭档弗兰克·莫斯利侦探，他，在一个会让查尔斯·狄更斯在坟墓里翻身的巧合中，恰好加入了这个旅游团来尝试寻宝。团队中的每个成员都有自己的关于宝藏真正性质以及如何找到它的理论，让加布里埃尔试图找出哪些真的只是看起来无望的业余爱好者，哪些有相关秘密要隐藏，可能涉及把他带到这里来的绑架案。

Anyone who has played the first two Gabriel Knight games will be familiar with this game's broad approach to storytelling. It takes place over the course of three days, with each day divided into a number of time blocks. The game runs on plot time rather than clock time: the clock only advances when you have fulfilled the requirements to end a time block.
任何玩过前两部《狩魔猎人》游戏的人都会熟悉这部游戏的故事 broad approach。它发生在三天内，每一天被分成若干个时间段。游戏运行的是情节时间而不是时钟时间：只有当你满足了结束一个时间段的要求时，时钟才会前进。

Grace arrives at the hotel on the first evening. Thereafter, you alternate between controlling her and Gabriel just as she does, as in Gabriel Knight 2, with Gabriel's segments focusing more on dialogue and physical investigation while Grace delves into the legends and conspiracy theories of Rennes-le-Château. Don't let the fact that the entire game is compressed into just three days fool you: these are busy days (and nights), busier than any three days in real life.
格蕾丝在第一天晚上抵达酒店。此后，你像她一样交替控制她和加布里埃尔，就像《狩魔猎人2》中一样，加布里埃尔的部分更侧重于对话和实际调查，而格蕾丝则深入研究雷恩-勒-夏托的传说和阴谋论。不要让整个游戏被压缩在仅仅三天内这一事实欺骗你：这是忙碌的三天（和夜晚），比现实生活中任何三天都要 busy。

I won't say any more about the mystery of Rennes-le-Château in this article. For now, you need only believe me when I tell you that it is an endlessly fascinating rabbit hole. In fact, it is fascinating on two separate levels: the tinfoil-hat theories themselves, and the meta-level of how they managed to find such purchase in our real world, which — spoiler alert! — is not actually controlled by secret cabals like the Knights Templar. I will explore these themes in some articles after this one. This is a departure from my normal beat, but I simply couldn't resist; I hope you'll agree in the end that it was well worth it.
在这篇文章中，我不会再说更多关于雷恩-勒-夏托的 mystery。目前，你只需要相信我告诉你，这是一个 endless fascinating 的兔子洞。事实上，它在两个 separate 层面上令人着迷：锡箔帽理论本身，以及关于它们如何在我们的现实世界中找到如此 purchase 的元层面，而这个世界——剧透警告！——实际上并不受圣殿骑士团等 secret cabals 的控制。我将在本文之后的一些文章中探讨这些主题。这偏离了我正常的 beat，但我就是无法抗拒；我希望你最终能同意这是非常值得的。

But let me tell you today about some other aspects of this game. One of Jane Jensen's greatest talents as a writer is her ability to evoke a sense of place, whether her backdrop is Louisiana, Bavaria, or now the south of France. If you ask me, this game is her magnum opus in that respect.
不过，今天让我告诉你关于这款游戏的其他一些方面。简·詹森作为作家最大的才能之一是她唤起地方感的能力，无论她的背景是路易斯安那、巴伐利亚，还是现在的法国南部。如果你问我，这款游戏是她这方面 magnum opus。

The 3D graphics here are fairly crude — far from state of the art even by the standards of 1999, let alone today. The characters move more like zombies or robots than real people, looking like collections of interchangeable parts crudely stitched together. Gabriel's hair looks like an awkwardly shaped helmet that might fall off his head at any moment, while the trees and plants are jagged pixel amalgamations that look like they might cut him if he bumped into them.
这里的3D图形相当 crude——即使按照1999年的标准也远非 state of the art，更不用说今天了。角色的移动更像僵尸或机器人而不是真人，看起来像是粗糙缝合在一起的 interchangeable parts 的集合。加布里埃尔的头发看起来像一个 awkwardly shaped 的头盔，随时可能从他头上掉下来，而树木和植物是锯齿状的像素 amalgamations，看起来如果他撞到它们，它们可能会把他切开。

And yet, damn it, playing this game really does feel like exploring a sun-drenched village on the edge of the French Riviera. Screenshots may not look very good in an article like this one, but the game's aesthetic has an impressionistic quality (how French, right?) that may actually serve it better than more photo-realistic graphics would. When I think back on it now, I almost think of it like a memorable vacation, the kind whose contours are blended and softened by memory's soothing hand. If a good game is a space you want to go to just to hang out, then Gabriel Knight 3 really is a very good game.
然而，该死的，玩这款游戏确实感觉像是在探索法国里维埃拉边缘一个阳光普照的村庄。截图在这样一篇文章中可能看起来不太好，但游戏的美学有一种印象派品质（多么法式，对吧？）可能实际上比更 photo-realistic 的图形更好地服务于它。当我现在回想它时，我几乎像是回想一次难忘的假期，那种轮廓被感情的 soothing hand 融合和 softens 的假期。如果一款好游戏是一个你想去只是 hang out 的空间，那么《狩魔猎人3》确实是一款非常好的游戏。

The geography is fairly constrained, meaning that you return to the same places again and again as the plot unfolds. Far from being a disadvantage, I find this oddly soothing. I mentioned Agatha Christie earlier; now let me double-down on that reference and say that the geography is compact enough to remind me of a locked-room cozy mystery. The fact that you are staying in a hotel with a gaggle of tourists only enhances the virtual vacation feeling. Playing this game, you never feel the stress, conflict, and exhaustion that its developers so often suffered. Call it another way that Gabriel Knight 3 was miraculous; most games reflect their circumstances of creation more indelibly.
地理相当 constrained，意味着随着情节展开，你会一次又一次地访问相同的地方。远非缺点，我发现这也 oddly soothing。我之前提到了阿加莎·克里斯蒂；现在让我 double-down 那个 reference，说地理足够紧凑，让我想起一个 locked-room cozy mystery。你和一个游客 gaggle 住在酒店里，这一事实 only enhances 虚拟度假的感觉。玩这款游戏，你从未感受到它的开发者经常遭受的压力、冲突和疲惫。称之为《狩魔猎人3》奇迹般的另一种方式；大多数游戏更 indelibly 反映其创作环境。

I suppose this might be considered a problem for Gabriel Knight 3 as a work of fiction: the setting is so pastoral when the stakes are supposed to be so high. But I don't care. I like it here; I really, really like it.
我想这可能被认为是《狩魔猎人3》作为一部小说作品的一个问题：当赌注应该如此之高时，背景却如此田园诗般。但我不在乎。我喜欢这里；我真的很喜欢。

Meet the story where it lives, let it unfold at its own pace, and you will be amply rewarded. The backstory of historical conspiracy and the foreground plot about finding the vampire kidnappers both become riveting. I often play games on the TV in the living room while my wife Dorthe reads, knits, or does other things, looking up from time to time to comment, usually to mock whatever nerdy thing I happen to be doing that evening.
在故事 live 的地方 meet 它，让它以自己的节奏展开，你会得到充分的回报。历史阴谋的背景故事和与之交织的前景情节，关于寻找吸血鬼绑架者，都变得引人入胜。我经常在客厅的电视上玩游戏，而我妻子多尔特则阅读、钩编或做其他事情，时不时抬头评论，通常是嘲笑我今晚碰巧在做的一些书呆子事情。

But Gabriel Knight 3 hooked her too, which doesn't happen often. She had to go away for a week-long course right when I was nearing the end. She explicitly told me that I was not allowed to finish without her, because she wanted to see the ending too. Believe me when I tell you, that really says something.
但《狩魔猎人3》也抓住了她，这并不经常发生。她不得不去参加一个为期一周的课程，就在我快接近尾声的时候。她明确告诉我，我不被允许在她不在的情况下完成，因为她也想看结局。相信我告诉你，那真的说明了一些事情。

The 3D engine that drives it all is one of many alternative approaches that popped up during the genre's flailing period against the fading mainstream limelight, designed to help it feel more current and, in some cases, to make it more suitable for alternative platforms like the Sony PlayStation. Few of these reinventions made a strong case for their own existence in my view, but the G-Engine is an exception. It is a surprisingly effective toolkit.
驱动这一切的3D引擎是众多 alternative approaches 之一，它们出现在该 genre 对抗主流光芒消逝的 flailing 时期，旨在帮助它感觉更符合时代，并在某些情况下，使其更适合索尼 PlayStation 等替代平台。这些 reinventions 中很少有在我看来为它们自己的存在提出强有力的 case，但G引擎是一个例外。它是一个 surprisingly effective 的工具包。

Unlike Grim Fandango, which relied on fixed camera angles in its 3D engine, Gabriel Knight 3 gives you a free-floating camera that can move about freely. The environment fills the whole screen; there are no fixed interface elements. Clicking on a hotspot pops up a context-sensitive menu of interaction possibilities. Of course, you can go into the inventory screen to examine and combine items you're carrying, or snatch them up for use in the world.
与《冥界狂想曲》在其3D引擎中依赖固定摄影角度不同，《狩魔猎人3》给你一个可以自由移动的 free-floating 摄影机。环境填满整个屏幕；没有固定的界面元素。点击一个热点会弹出一个 context-sensitive 的 interaction possibilities 菜单。当然，你可以深入 inventory screen 查看和组合你携带的物品，或将它们 snatch up 用于世界中的使用。

I really, really like this system, and it does add something extra that you couldn't get from 2D pixel graphics. You can look up and down, left and right, look under and over things. A room suddenly feels like a real space, full of nooks and crannies waiting to be explored.
我真的、非常喜欢这个系统，它确实添加了从2D像素图形中无法获得的额外东西。你可以上下左右看，看东西的下面和上面。一个房间突然感觉像一个真实的空间，充满了 nook and cranny 等待探索。

Admittedly, the setup is a bit strange on a conceptual level, because you're doing all this exploration while Gabriel or Grace — whichever one you happen to be controlling at the moment — stands perfectly still. In other words, this game lends new credence to Scott Adams's ancient concept of the adventure gamer commanding a "puppet" to carry out her orders. Here, you are a disembodied spirit doing all the real work, only commandeering Gabriel or Grace when you need hands, feet, or a mouth.
诚然，这个概念层面的设置有点奇怪，因为你在做所有这些探索时，加布里埃尔或格蕾丝——你碰巧正在控制的那个——却一动不动地站着。换句话说，这款游戏为斯科特·亚当斯关于冒险游戏玩家指挥一个"木偶"来执行她的命令的古老概念提供了新的可信度。在这里，你是一个 disembodied spirit，做所有真正的工作，只有在需要手、脚或嘴时才征用加布里埃尔或格蕾丝。

You can even "examine" an object in a room without their help — doing so shows it to you in close-up — although you do need them to "look" at it for you, which elicits a verbal description from your puppet. Gabriel Knight 3 does not take place in a contiguous world; discrete "rooms" load in as you command your puppet to cross the boundaries from one to another. Some rooms can be quite large, however. When you're out on the streets of Rennes-le-Château, for example, the camera may be a block away from Gabriel or Grace, well out of his or her line of sight. It seems strange when you think about it, but it works well in practice.
你甚至可以在没有他们帮助的情况下"检查"房间里的一个物体——这样做会以 close-up 展示给你——尽管你确实需要他们来帮你"看"，这会从你的木偶那里引出口头描述。《狩魔猎人3》并不发生在一个 contiguous 的世界中；离散的"房间"在你指挥你的木偶从一个跨越边界到另一个时加载进来。然而，有些房间可能相当大。当你在雷恩-勒-夏托的街道上外出时，例如，摄影机可能距离加布里埃尔或格蕾丝有一个街区远，well out of his or her line of sight。想起来很奇怪，但在实践中效果很好。

One of the strangest things about the G-Engine is that the camera seems to have a corporeal form. You can get it stuck behind objects like benches.
G引擎最奇怪的事情之一是摄影机似乎有一个 corporeal form。你可以让它被卡在长椅等物体后面。

The G-Engine doesn't add much in the way of emergent possibility. Reading between the lines of some reviews, one senses that some critics thought the move to 3D should have made Gabriel Knight 3 play more like Tomb Raider — who knows, maybe that was even what the developers envisioned at some point. What we have, however, is very much an adventure game of the old school, a series of set-piece puzzles with set-piece solutions, a set-piece plot fated to unfold in one and only one way.
G引擎在 emergent possibility 方面并没有增加太多。阅读一些评论的字里行间，人们不禁 sense 一些评论家认为转向3D应该让《狩魔猎人3》玩起来更像《古墓丽影》——谁知道呢，也许这甚至曾经是开发者在某个时候 envision 的。然而，我们拥有的游戏非常 much an adventure game of the old school，一系列 set-piece puzzles with set-piece solutions，一个 set-piece plot 注定要以一种且只有一种方式展开。

Some alternate solutions are provided, even some optional paths and puzzles you can engage with for extra points, but there's no physics engine to speak of, and absolutely no possibility of doing anything Jane Jensen never anticipated you might do. That said, there are a few places where the game demands timing and reflexes, particularly in the climax. These sections aren't horrible, but they're also unlikely to make you wish for more. Ultimately, they're set-piece exercises too, more Dragon's Lair than Tomb Raider.
提供了一些替代解决方案，甚至一些可选的路径和谜题，你可以参与其中以获得额外分数，但这里没有什么 physics engine 可言，也绝对没有做任何简·詹森从未预料到你要做的事情的可能性。也就是说，有几个地方游戏要求时机和反应，特别是在 climax。这些部分并不 horrible，但它们也不太可能让你希望有更多。最终，它们也是 set-piece exercises，更像《龙穴历险记》而不是《古墓丽影》。

They created fictional teenage personas for themselves, Erik a "fat, girl-looking boy" and Chet a kid who "likes Ministry and nothing else." How meta, right?
埃里克·沃尔波和切特·法利塞克通过他们的网站Old Man Murray以所有错误的原因将《狩魔猎人3》写入了游戏历史。他们为自己创造了虚构的青少年 persona，埃里克是一个"胖的、看起来像女孩的男孩"，切特是一个"喜欢Ministry和没别的"的孩子。多么 meta，对吧？

This was not a piece of satire for the ages, but it went viral nonetheless, until it seemed to be everywhere on the Internet. In an ironic, roundabout way, Gabriel Knight 3's cat-hair mustache puzzle became one of the most famous puzzles in all of adventure-game history, up there with the babel fish from The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy or the insult swordfighting from Monkey Island. It became so famous that today it has its own Wikipedia page.
这不是一篇穿透时代的讽刺作品，但它仍然像病毒一样传播，直到它似乎在互联网上无处不在。以一种讽刺的、间接的方式，《狩魔猎人3》的猫毛胡子谜题成为所有冒险游戏历史上最著名的谜题之一，与《银河系漫游指南》中的巴别鱼或《猴岛》的侮辱剑术相提并论。它变得如此著名，以至于今天它有自己的维基百科页面。

Meanwhile, it became the Exhibit A in the burgeoning debate about "why adventure games died." To this day, whenever the conversation turns to the genre's decline at the end of the twentieth century, a reference to the cat-hair mustache cannot long be absent. For a significant number of people today, Gabriel Knight 3 is not a game about vampires or Da Vinci Code-style material before Dan Brown discovered it; it is the game about the cat-hair mustache.
同时，它成为关于"冒险游戏为何死亡"的 burgeoning debate 的头号展品。直到今天，每当话题转向该 genre 在二十世纪末的衰落时，对猫毛胡子的 reference 就不能长久缺席。对于今天相当数量的人来说，《狩魔猎人3》不是一款关于吸血鬼或丹·布朗发现之前的《达·芬奇密码》素材的游戏；它是一款关于猫毛胡子的游戏。

So what's a Gabriel Knight 3 apologist like me to do? First, let me acknowledge that, from a strictly mimetic standpoint, this is not a great puzzle, because it cannot be taken seriously as part of the game fiction. Putting aside all the other improbable steps Gabriel has to go through, he steals and defaces — more on the latter shortly — his good friend's passport to pull off this scam. All of this rather than, you know, just asking his buddy for help. (Mosely is gruff on the outside, but a softie at heart, as Gabriel knows better than anyone.) Or he could suck it up and ride the moped for a few days, considering that the fate of an innocent baby and who knows what else is depending on this.
那么，像我这样的《狩魔猎人3》辩护者该如何应对呢？首先，让我承认，从严格 mimetic 的角度来看，这不是一个伟大的谜题，因为作为游戏 fiction 的一部分，它不可能被认真对待。把加布里埃尔必须经历的所有其他 improbable 步骤放在一边，他偷走并 defaces——稍后再说后者——他好朋友的护照来进行这个骗局。所有这一切，而不是，你知道，直接请求他的伙伴帮忙。（莫斯利外表粗犷，但内心深处是个好人，这一点加布里埃尔比任何人都清楚。）或者他可以 suck it up 骑几天助动车，考虑到一个无辜婴儿的命运以及谁知道还有什么可能取决于此。

Of course, Gabriel Knight 3 is far from the only adventure game to feature sociopathic behavior in its puzzle tree as a staple. This goes to the root of the genre's centripetal pull toward comedy, which almost always injects a patina of goofiness into allegedly serious games like this one. This stuff is better suited to games like Monkey Island. But if you leave it out entirely from any form of adventure, you risk having a game that isn't content-rich enough. If we're not afraid of a little whataboutism, we might mount a defense of adventure games here by pointing out that it's far from the only genre whose gameplay so often conflicts with its fiction: think of putting the saving of the world on hold in CRPGs to look for lost pets and complete a hundred other trivial side quests, or researching the same technologies over and over again from scratch in RTS campaigns. I'll leave you to decide how compelling such a defense is.
当然，《狩魔猎人3》远非唯一一款将其谜题树的 sociopathic behavior 作为 staple 的冒险游戏。这是该 genre 向喜剧 centripetal pull 的根源，它几乎总是将一层 goofiness 的 patina 注入像这部这样的 allegedly serious 游戏中。这种东西更适合像《猴岛》这样的游戏。但如果在任何形式的冒险中完全 leave it out，你就有可能拥有一款游戏内容不够丰富的游戏。如果我们不怕一点 whataboutism，我们可能会在这里为冒险游戏辩护，指出它远非唯一一个其游戏玩法经常与其 fiction 冲突的 genre：想想在CRPG中为了寻找丢失的宠物和完成一百个其他微不足道的支线任务而将拯救世界搁置一旁，或者在RTS战役中一遍又一遍地从头研究相同的技术。我让你自己决定这样的辩护有多 compelling。

For what it's worth, there are reports that this puzzle was not in Jane Jensen's original design, that it was substituted later for another that proved impractical to implement. Rest assured, you won't hear me calling it a great puzzle, either in the context of this game or in adventure-game history.
据 worth 的说法，有报告称这个谜题不在简·詹森的 original design 中，它是在后来替换另一个证明 impractical to implement 的谜题的。放心，你不会听到我称它为伟大的谜题，无论是在这部游戏的背景下还是在冒险游戏历史上。

But here's the thing: setting mimesis aside, it's not nearly as terrible as our friend Erik made it out to be. I played this game for the first time a few months ago, knowing vaguely that it included an infamous puzzle involving a cat-hair mustache — how could I not know? — but knowing nothing of the specifics. I went into it fully expecting the worst, mindful of the design issues I remembered from the first two Gabriel Knight games. Thus I was surprised to discover how smoothly the entire puzzle played for me — yes, enjoyably even.
但问题是：撇开 mimesis 不谈，它远不如我们朋友埃里克描述的那样 terrible。几个月前我第一次玩这款游戏，模糊地知道它包括一个涉及猫毛胡子的 infamous puzzle——我怎么能不知道呢？——但对 specifics 一无所知。我 fully expecting the worst 进入游戏，牢记我从前两部《狩魔猎人》游戏中记得的设计问题。因此，当我发现整个谜题对我来说玩得多么顺利——是的，甚至 enjoyably——时，我很惊讶。

Perhaps the knowledge I brought into the game helped, but I never felt like I was relying on it, never felt like I couldn't have progressed without it. There are two hugely important mitigating factors that Erik neglects to mention.
也许我进入游戏时带来的知识帮了忙，但我从未觉得我在依赖它，从未觉得没有它我就无法 progress。有两个 hugely important mitigating factors，埃里克 neglects to mention。

The first is that there is a logic to Gabriel making himself a mustache to impersonate the clean-shaven (more or less) Mosely. He believes that facial hair can disguise the two men's very different facial bone structures. Thus he draws a mustache on Mosely's passport photo with a marker — want to have a friend like him? — to complete the deception.
第一个是，加布里埃尔为自己制作胡子来模仿 clean-shaven（或多或少）的莫斯利，实际上是有逻辑的。他认为面部毛发可以掩饰两人非常不同的面部骨骼结构。因此他用马克笔在莫斯利的护照照片上画了胡子——你想有一个像他这样的朋友吗？——来完成 deception。

Note the mustache that now appears on Mosely's passport photo.
注意莫斯利的护照照片上现在有胡子了。

The second factor is more thoroughgoing: the player is led through all the steps quite explicitly by Gabriel himself. (Who's the puppet now, right?) In addition to "look" and "examine," many hotspots pop up a handy lightbulb icon when clicked: "think." These provide important guidance about, well, what your character is thinking — or rather, what Jane Jensen is thinking, what avenues she expects you to explore to advance the story.
第二个因素更 thoroughgoing：玩家通过加布里埃尔自己相当明确地被引导完成所有步骤。（现在谁是木偶了，对吧？）除了"看"和"检查"之外，许多热点在点击时会弹出一个方便的灯泡图标："思考"。这些提供关于，well，你的角色在想什么——或者更确切地说，简·詹森在想什么，她期望你探索哪些途径来推进故事的重要指导。

This is not a walkthrough — what fun would that be? — but it does give you the outline of what you're trying to accomplish. In this case, carefully looking at and "thinking" about all the objects involved turns what would really be an absurdly unfair puzzle without this extra information into one that is silly on its face, yes, but fun all the same. I've ranted plenty in the past about bad adventure-game puzzles, the kind where you don't know what the game wants you to do or how it wants you to do it. This is not one of them. This puzzle doesn't deserve the eternal infamy that Old Man Murray draped over it.
这不是一个 walkthrough——那有什么乐趣？——但它确实给了你你试图 accomplish 的轮廓。在这种情况下，仔细查看并"思考"所有涉及的对象，将一个如果没有这些额外信息真的会 absurdly unfair 的谜题，变成一个 face 上很傻，是的，但 all the same 很有趣的谜题。我过去对糟糕的冒险游戏谜题 rant 过 plenty，那种你不知道游戏想让你做什么或想让你怎么做的谜题。这不是其中之一。这个谜题不应该得到 Old Man Murray 给它披上的 eternal infamy。

In fact, Gabriel Knight 3 represents a major leap forward over the first two games in terms of pure design. While it is by no means trivial to solve, neither does it seem to hate its player the way so many older Sierra games did. The "think" verb is one example. Here's another: once you've solved the cat-hair mustache puzzle, ridden your illegally obtained Harley, and started visiting the sites around Rennes-le-Château, you can start having the game show you where else you still need to accomplish things in the current time block; this alone goes a long way toward alleviating the "fumbling around in limbo" feeling Tom Chick described in Computer Gaming World.
事实上，《狩魔猎人3》在纯设计方面比前两部游戏有了重大飞跃。虽然它绝非轻而易举就能 solve，但它也不像许多 older Sierra 游戏那样似乎恨它的玩家。"思考"动词就是一个例子。还有另一个例子：一旦你解决了猫毛胡子谜题，骑上你非法获得的哈雷，开始参观雷恩-勒-夏托周围的地方，你可以开始让游戏向你展示在当前时间段你还需要在哪里完成事情；仅此一点就大大缓解了汤姆·奇克在《电脑游戏世界》中描述的"在 limbo 中摸索"的感觉。

I don't know whether this more soluble design is the result of Jane Jensen's improving craft, or that of unsung heroes on her team, or possibly even directives from dreaded upper management. I only know that it's really, really good — a delightful surprise to find a game that is actually better than its reputation.
我不知道这款游戏的 more soluble design 是简·詹森技艺提高的结果，还是她与团队合作的无名英雄的结果，甚至可能来自 dreaded upper management 的 directives。我只知道它真的、真的很好——惊喜地发现一款游戏实际上比它的 reputation 更好。

Solving the Le Serpent Rouge puzzle. Jane Jensen cribbed all this "sacred geometry" business from The Tomb of God, which in turn borrowed it from Renaissance and early-modern Hermetic philosophy. (Johannes Kepler was quite fond of this stuff when he wasn't developing the first credible model of our solar system's heliocentric structure.) It may be nonsense, but it's wonderfully evocative nonsense when it's embedded in a story like this one.
解决Le Serpent Rouge谜题。简·詹森从《上帝的坟墓》一书中抄袭了所有这些关于"神圣几何"的 business，而这本书又是从文艺复兴和早期现代赫尔墨斯主义哲学中借来的。（约翰内斯·开普勒在开发我们太阳系日心说模型的第一个可信模型时之外，非常喜欢这种东西。）这可能是胡说八道，但当它嵌入到像这样的故事中时，它是 wonderfully evocative 的胡说八道。

The other puzzles here are a commodious grab bag of types. Some are as silly as the cat-hair mustache, but most are more pertinent to the actual mysteries you're trying to solve. Among the most elaborately designed of them is a whole chain of puzzles that become Grace's main focus on the second and third days, remembered almost as well in hardcore adventure circles as the cat-hair mustache is outside of them. The Le Serpent Rouge puzzle sequence is named after a 1967 poem by an anonymous author, which has become an indelible part of the Rennes-le-Château conspiracy legend.
这里的其他谜题是一个 commodious grab bag of types。其中一些和猫毛胡子一样傻，但大多数更 pertinent to 你实际试图解决的 mysteries。其中最精心设计的谜题是一整套谜题链，成为格蕾丝在第二和第三天的主要 focus，它们在硬核冒险圈中的记忆度几乎和猫毛胡子在圈外的记忆度一样高。Le Serpent Rouge 谜题序列得名于1967年一位匿名作者的一首诗，这首诗已成为雷恩-勒-夏托阴谋传说中不可磨灭的一部分。

The reliably bookish Grace must use various software tools on her laptop to dig out its encoded meanings section by section. Some reviewers called it the best adventure-game puzzle ever devised. For my part, I can't go quite that far. Like everything else in the G-Engine, the software Grace uses is more veneer on set-piece design than real simulation. At several stages, I more or less just clicked on stuff until the game told me I had done it right.
 reliably bookish 的格蕾丝必须使用她笔记本电脑上的各种软件工具，逐节挖掘其编码含义。一些评论家称它为有史以来最好的冒险游戏谜题。就我而言，我不能 go quite that far。像G引擎中的其他一切一样，格蕾丝使用的软件更像是 set-piece design 上的 veneer，而不是真正的 simulation。在几个阶段，我 more or less 只是点击东西直到游戏告诉我我做对了。

But if it has limitations as a set of pure puzzles, Le Serpent Rouge succeeds brilliantly as interactive drama. By the time it appears, you are fully invested, and the excitement you feel as you step closer to the heart of the mystery is not to be sneezed at. In a more just world, it would be these puzzles rather than the cat-hair mustache puzzle that hold a place in mainstream gaming lore. Because they show how exciting and gripping intelligent, textured, context-dependent adventure-game puzzles can be.
但如果它作为一组 pure puzzles 有局限性，Le Serpent Rouge 作为互动戏剧取得了辉煌的成功。当它出现时，你已经 fully invested，当你一步步接近 mystery 的核心时，你得到的兴奋感是不应被轻视的。在一个更公正的世界里，应该是这些谜题而不是猫毛胡子谜题在主流游戏传说中占有一席之地。因为它们展示了智能的、有质感的、与上下文相关的冒险游戏谜题可以多么令人兴奋和扣人心弦。

The same sentiment can be applied to Gabriel Knight 3 as a whole, a rare Sierra adventure game that I find to be underrated rather than overrated. I haven't always been so kind to Sierra's games, as many of you are well aware. But almost twenty years after they first started, right before they turned the lights off forever, they finally got everything right. This game is my favorite from the entire Sierra catalog. It is the opposite of Ultima IX, the understated farewell to match that game's understated swan song. Here's to lost causes and eleventh-hour miracles.
同样的情感也可以应用于《狩魔猎人3》整体，一部我发现被低估而不是被高估的罕见的雪乐山冒险游戏。我并不总是对雪乐山的游戏如此友善，正如你们中的许多人非常清楚的那样。但近二十年后，就在他们永远熄灯之前，他们终于把一切都做对了。这款游戏是我整个雪乐山目录中最喜欢的。它是《创世纪IX》的对立面，就像那部游戏的低调一样，这部游戏是高调的谢幕。让我们为 lost causes 和 eleventh-hour miracles 欢呼。

Girls can ride Harleys too, folks.
女孩们也可以骑哈雷，各位。

I have to admit that my experience with Gabriel Knight 3 has caused me to reevaluate the entire series to which it belongs. As a bloody-minded iconoclast, I find I must rank the games in reverse chronological order, the opposite of the typical fan's order. I still can't fully get behind Gabriel Knight 1, even when I try to separate the story and setting from my nightmares of hunting for two-pixel-wide snake scales in Bayou swamps and tapping out nonsense codes on bongo drums. But Gabriel Knight 2... that's really a borderline case for me.
我不得不承认，我与《狩魔猎人3》的经历在某种程度上让我重新评估了它所归属的整个系列。作为一个 bloody-minded iconoclast，我发现我必须按相反的时间顺序对游戏进行排名，这与典型粉丝的顺序相反。我仍然无法完全支持《狩魔猎人1》，即使当我试图将故事和背景与我在 Bayou 沼泽中寻找两像素宽的蛇鳞和在邦戈鼓上敲击无意义代码的噩梦分开时。但《狩魔猎人2》……对我来说真的是一个边缘案例。

I still have a bunch of quibbles with its design, but its flaws are certainly less egregious than those of its predecessor, even if the way it has lingered in my memory is something I can say about few of the narrative-oriented games I've played for these histories. Both the second and third games give me an emotion that is not primary-colored, more textured and complex than love and hate, fight and flight. That is not to be sneezed at in the medium of video games.
我仍然对它的设计有一堆 quibbles，但它的缺陷 certainly less egregious than those of its predecessor，即使它在我的记忆中的留存方式是我在为这些历史玩过的叙事导向游戏中很少有能做到的。第二和第三部游戏都让我感受到的情感不是 primary-colored，比爱和恨、战斗和逃跑更有质感和复杂。这在电子游戏媒介中是不应被 sneezed at 的。

So, readers, I suppose I must place both Gabriel Knight 2 and Gabriel Knight 3 in my personal hall of fame. The former was a long time coming, but I've made my peace with old Gabe and Gracie at last. Just too bad their story had to end here, just when it was getting juicy.
所以，读者们，我想我必须把《狩魔猎人2》和《狩魔猎人3》都放入我的个人名人堂。前者是一个 long time coming，但我最终还是接受了老加布和格雷西。只是太糟糕了，他们的故事不得不在这里结束，就在它开始变得 juicy 的时候。

Ooh-la-la!
哦啦啦！

## 批判性思考与评论 / Critical Thinking Commentary

### 作者核心论点分析

本文作者通过详细回顾《狩魔猎人3》的开发历程，试图为这款被历史"冤枉"的游戏正名。其核心论点可以归纳为三点：

1. **个人愿景的重要性**：作者反复强调简·詹森"从头到尾坚守项目现场"是游戏成功的关键，这与《创世纪IX》中理查德·加里奥特的缺席形成鲜明对比。作者认为，正是这种对创作者愿景的坚持，使得游戏在恶劣的开发环境中依然保持了艺术完整性。

2. **历史评价的偏见性**：作者敏锐地指出，1999年的游戏评论界已经形成了一种"冒险游戏已死"的集体心态，这种pack mentality导致《狩魔猎人3》遭受了不公正的评价。作者通过对比《冥界狂想曲》和《狩魔猎人3》的媒体 reception，揭示了评论界双重标准的存在。

3. **游戏设计的进步性**：作者力排众议，认为《狩魔猎人3》在谜题设计、叙事结构和氛围营造上都超越了前两部作品，特别是"思考"系统的引入和对玩家更友好的引导机制。

### 文章的优点

1. **丰富的历史细节**：文章基于大量一手资料，包括斯科特·比拉斯在《游戏开发者》杂志上的postmortem、开发团队的内部描述等，为读者呈现了一幅完整的开发地狱图景。这些细节不仅增加了文章的可信度，也让读者能够深入理解游戏制作的复杂性。

2. **有力的对比论证**：将《狩魔猎人3》与《创世纪IX》进行对比是一个非常有效的策略。两款游戏面临相似的困境却走向不同的结局，这种对比有力地证明了"愿景"和"领导力"在游戏开发中的决定性作用。

3. **对"猫毛胡子"谜题的重新审视**：作者没有简单地回避这个臭名昭著的谜题，而是勇敢地直面它，通过细致的文本分析指出这个谜题实际上并没有Old Man Murray描述的那么糟糕。这种辩护虽然可能引发争议，但体现了作者作为评论家的独立精神。

4. **情感真挚的个人体验**：作者分享妻子多尔特也被游戏吸引的轶事，虽然主观，但生动地传达了游戏在叙事层面的魅力。这种个人化的叙述方式为技术分析增添了温度。

### 文章的弱点

1. **选择性的证据使用**：虽然作者为"猫毛胡子"谜题辩护时指出了两个缓解因素（逻辑合理性和游戏内引导），但他似乎有意淡化了这个谜题在叙事逻辑上的根本问题——为什么加布里埃尔不直接找莫斯利帮忙？作者承认这是"mimesis"层面的问题，但很快用"冒险游戏普遍存在这种叙事与玩法的冲突"来开脱，这种辩护略显薄弱。

2. **对开发成本问题的回避**：文章提到游戏最终成本从150万美元飙升至420万美元，但没有深入分析这种超支对雪乐山公司整体命运的影响。作为雪乐山的"最后一部冒险游戏"，《狩魔猎人3》是否在某种程度上加速了公司的衰落？作者没有探讨这个更宏观的问题。

3. **可能存在的美化倾向**：作者坦承《狩魔猎人3》是他最喜欢的三部曲作品，这种个人偏好可能影响了他对游戏缺陷的评估。例如，他对游戏"印象派"画质的辩护——"比更照片级真实的图形更好地服务于它"——可能更多是一种善意的解读而非客观评价。

4. **对历史语境的简化**：虽然作者提到了1999年游戏市场的变化（第一人称射击和即时战略的崛起），但他似乎将《狩魔猎人3》的商业失败主要归咎于评论界的偏见，而对更深层的结构性因素——如冒险游戏这种形式与新兴玩家群体需求之间的根本性错位——讨论不够充分。

### 我的批判性视角

作为一名读者，我认为这篇文章最可贵的价值在于它提醒我们：**历史评价往往是由复杂的偶然因素塑造的，而非单纯的质量反映**。《狩魔猎人3》的案例生动地展示了"时机"在游戏历史中的重要性——一款游戏发行的年份、月份，甚至是评论界当时的集体情绪，都可能对其历史地位产生深远影响。

然而，我也认为作者可能过于急于为《狩魔猎人3》"翻案"。虽然他对Old Man Murray的批评有一定道理（那个著名的"猫毛胡子"吐槽确实有其夸张和表演性的一面），但这并不意味着这个谜题就没有问题。一个需要偷朋友护照、用枫糖浆粘猫毛胡子来假扮他的谜题，即使在最好的情况下，也是对叙事沉浸感的严重破坏。

更重要的是，我认为文章触及但没有充分展开的一个主题是：**商业游戏开发中艺术愿景与市场现实之间的永恒张力**。简·詹森的坚持固然令人敬佩，但在一个成本飙升至420万美元、团队士气崩溃的项目中，这种坚持是否也带有某种悲剧性的固执？如果《狩魔猎人3》真的如作者所说是一部杰作，那么为什么它需要在这样的混乱中诞生？这些问题指向了游戏产业更深层的不健康结构，而非仅仅是个别项目的成败。

### 启示与反思

这篇文章引发了一个更广泛的思考：**我们如何评价那些被历史"遗忘"或"误解"的作品？** 作者的答案似乎是：通过回到文本本身，剥离时代偏见的层层累积，重新发现作品本身的品质。这种方法有其价值，但也存在风险——我们是否应该用今天的标准去重新评价过去的作品？还是应当承认，作品的意义总是在特定的历史语境中生成的？

《狩魔猎人3》的命运或许也可以看作是整个冒险游戏genre的缩影：在技术变革和玩家口味转变的浪潮中，一种曾经主导的游戏形式如何艰难地寻找自己的位置。作者为《狩魔猎人3》写的这篇"辩护词"，在某种程度上也是为整个冒险游戏genre写的挽歌——不是悲伤的哀叹，而是一种充满敬意的告别，向那些在逆境中依然坚持创作的人们致敬。

最后，我认为这篇文章最有价值的启示可能是：**伟大的作品有时恰恰诞生于最不可能的环境**。无论是《狩魔猎人3》还是其他艺术史上的类似案例，它们提醒我们，人的创造力有时能够超越环境的限制，在最黑暗的时刻绽放出意想不到的光芒。这或许就是作者所说的"奇迹"的真正含义。
