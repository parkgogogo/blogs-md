By Taylor Rayne

作者：Taylor Rayne

Smell is our most primal and, arguably, most emotionally potent sense. It summons memories, shapes taste, and influences behavior: the aroma of coffee is capable of enhancing alertness well before caffeine ever reaches the bloodstream. A hint of sunscreen collapses decades, taking us back to youth; but, pinch the nose, and suddenly a slice of apple is hard to distinguish from a piece of raw potato.

嗅觉是我们最原始、也可能是最具情感力量的感官。它能唤起记忆、塑造味觉、影响行为：咖啡的香气能够在咖啡因进入血液之前就增强警觉性。一丝防晒霜的味道能让几十年的时光瞬间崩塌，带我们回到青春岁月；但捏住鼻子，突然间一片苹果就很难与一块生土豆区分开来。

Despite its significance, scent remains the most mysterious of our senses. Unlike vision or hearing, it resists straightforward formalization. The challenge lies not only in the vast molecular diversity of odorants, which vary in far more ways than photons or frequencies, but also in the effort to build a shared vocabulary and technology capable of codifying subjective sensation. So while machines have learned to see through computer vision and hear through signal processing, scent remains stubbornly analog. There has been no RGB of odor, no Fourier transform for smell.

尽管嗅觉如此重要，它仍然是我们最神秘的感官。与视觉或听觉不同，它难以被直接形式化。挑战不仅在于气味分子巨大的多样性——它们的变化方式远比光子或频率复杂——还在于建立能够编码主观感受的共享词汇和技术体系。因此，当机器已经学会通过计算机视觉看见、通过信号处理听见时，嗅觉仍然顽固地保持模拟状态。气味没有RGB标准，也没有傅里叶变换。

At least, until now.

至少，直到现在。

Tech giants, including Google, startups such as Osmo, and even traditional fragrance houses like Givaudan have begun turning to AI to probe the possibility of digitizing smell. By encoding scent molecules into 1s and 0s, their hope is to better understand and manipulate this sensory modality. Just as "computer vision" has helped us realize that sight is not just passive image capture but an active process of prediction and interpretation, researchers hope that programming smell will illuminate the many mysteries of olfaction.

包括谷歌在内的科技巨头、Osmo这样的初创公司，甚至像奇华顿这样的传统香水公司，都开始转向人工智能来探索数字化嗅觉的可能性。通过将气味分子编码为1和0，他们希望更好地理解和操纵这种感官模式。正如"计算机视觉"帮助我们认识到视觉不仅仅是被动地捕捉图像，而是预测和解释的主动过程，研究人员希望编程嗅觉能够揭示嗅觉的诸多奥秘。

Beyond providing further insight into olfactory biology, digital scent could have many practical (and quite profitable) applications, which is why its proponents, from defense agencies such as DARPA to corporate conglomerates like Estée Lauder, have invested in it. Computational smell could, for example, help detect threats and information invisible to cameras, such as gas leaks, food spoilage, disease markers in breath, and even counterfeit products. It could also reduce reliance on resource-intensive natural ingredients used in perfume and other odorants by, for instance, finding chemically synthesized molecules capable of evoking the same brain patterns. And finally, it could lead to the creation of entirely novel smells, revealing a vast, untapped chemical palette that would otherwise be unattainable without the aid of technology.

除了提供对嗅觉生物学的深入理解，数字嗅觉还可能有许多实用（且相当有利可图）的应用，这就是为什么从DARPA这样的国防机构到雅诗兰黛这样的企业集团都对其进行了投资。例如，计算嗅觉可以帮助检测相机无法捕捉到的威胁和信息，如煤气泄漏、食物变质、呼吸中的疾病标志物，甚至假冒产品。它还可以通过寻找能够引发相同大脑模式的化学合成分子，减少香水和其他气味剂对资源密集型天然成分的依赖。最后，它可能带来全新气味的创造，揭示一个广阔、未开发的化学调色板，这些在没有技术辅助的情况下是无法获得的。

Long before life evolved eyes and ears, the world was encountered chemically. This took place as molecules permeated and diffused across cell membranes, performing a metabolic exchange between animate and inanimate matter.

早在生命进化出眼睛和耳朵之前，世界就是以化学方式被感知的。这一过程发生在分子渗透并扩散穿过细胞膜时，在生物与非生物物质之间进行代谢交换。

Smell, our most ancient interface with the environment, originated over 3 billion years ago, in bacteria adrift in the primordial ocean. These early organisms navigated chemical gradients in the water, detecting molecules to swim toward food and away from danger. This ability, known as chemosensation, is the most rudimentary form of smell.

嗅觉，作为我们与最古老的环境界面，起源于30多亿年前，在原始海洋中漂浮的细菌里。这些早期生物在水中导航化学梯度，探测分子以游向食物、远离危险。这种能力被称为化学感觉，是最原始的嗅觉形式。

Such "sensing" relies on receptor proteins embedded in the cell membrane, acting like molecular locks awaiting the corresponding chemical key. When a passing odor molecule fits into a receptor's binding site, it changes the receptor's shape, setting off a cascade of signals inside the cell that direct the organism's movement.

这种"感知"依赖于嵌入细胞膜中的受体蛋白，就像等待对应化学钥匙的分子锁。当一个路过的气味分子适配到受体的结合位点时，它会改变受体的形状，在细胞内引发一连串信号，指导生物体的运动。

Over time, these molecules didn't just guide survival; they encouraged multicellular life. As cells began clustering together, the exchange of semiochemicals — molecular signals that transmit information within and between species — began to influence behavior, enabling aggregation and synchronization, and laying the groundwork for cellular cooperation. Plants, for instance, release green leaf volatiles such as cis-3-hexenal (the familiar scent of freshly cut grass) when attacked, both warning neighboring plants and attracting the animals that prey on herbivores. Human infants, meanwhile, are drawn to the distinctive odor of their mother's milk, which both stimulates feeding and regulates their earliest physiological rhythms. And among insects, ants are famous for deploying pheromones such as iridodials, which direct entire colonies to forage or fight in concert.

随着时间的推移，这些分子不仅指导生存；它们促进了多细胞生命的形成。当细胞开始聚集在一起时，信息化学物质——在物种内部和之间传递信息的分子信号——的交换开始影响行为，实现聚集和同步，为细胞合作奠定基础。例如，植物在受到攻击时会释放绿叶挥发物，如顺-3-己烯醛（新鲜割草的气味），既警告邻近植物，又吸引捕食草食动物的动物。同时，人类婴儿会被母亲乳汁的独特气味吸引，这既刺激进食，又调节他们最早的生理节律。而在昆虫中，蚂蚁以部署信息素如虹彩醛而闻名，它们指导整个群体协同觅食或战斗。

Once the first tetrapods emerged from the sea and embraced life on land, smelling evolved to become ever more refined under newfound terrestrial pressures, including adapting to novel volatile odorants and the more variable conditions of airborne climates. As these early terrestrial vertebrates expanded a chemosensory repertoire that had once been far more limited in their aquatic ancestors, olfactory systems likewise continued to evolve. Over the next hundreds of millions of years, the neural structures supporting our own sense of smell increased in sophistication.

一旦第一批四足动物从海洋中出现并适应陆地生活，嗅觉在新兴的陆地压力下变得更加精细，包括适应新的挥发性气味剂和更多变的大气气候条件。随着这些早期陆生脊椎动物扩展了在其水生祖先中曾经更加有限的化学感官库，嗅觉系统也继续进化。在接下来的数亿年里，支持我们嗅觉的神经结构变得更加复杂。

Olfactory neurons in the nasal cavity bind to odorants and pass signals into the brain. Different combinations of activated receptors are perceived as different scents.

鼻腔中的嗅觉神经元与气味剂结合，将信号传递到大脑。不同组合的激活受体被感知为不同的气味。

Today, roughly two to five percent of our genetic blueprint concerns itself with smell. While it may seem a small fraction of the whole, it is the largest gene family in the human genome. At any given time, 77 percent of the 356 distinct olfactory receptors are expressed in the lining of the nasal cavity, each tuned to the molecules that make up the world's myriad smells. The receptors expressed vary between individuals, with only 90 receptors commonly found in all people. Together, this suite of receptors is responsible for every experience of scent we encounter, and the variability between individuals is likely behind smells' subjectivity.

今天，我们基因蓝图的大约2%到5%与嗅觉有关。虽然这看起来只是整体的一小部分，但它是人类基因组中最大的基因家族。在任何给定时间，356种不同嗅觉受体中的77%在鼻腔内衬中表达，每种都针对构成世界上无数气味的分子。表达的受体因人而异，只有90种受体在所有人中都普遍存在。这些受体共同负责我们遇到的每一次嗅觉体验，而个体之间的差异很可能是嗅觉主观性的根源。

To understand how this olfactory complexity works, consider the single inhalation that follows biting into a ripe strawberry: the rush of aroma, the sweet, tangy burst blooming in the nose, created not by a single compound but by a volatile molecular cocktail. There is no one single molecule responsible for the smell of strawberry, but rather a family — namely furaneol (caramel-sweet), methyl and ethyl butanoate (fruity), methyl 2-propanoate (fruity), hexanal (green and sharp), and cis-3-hexenol (leafy-fresh) — that evaporate and surge through the nasal cavity.

为了理解这种嗅觉复杂性是如何运作的，想象一下咬下一颗成熟草莓后的那次吸气：香气的冲击，甜美、酸爽的爆发在鼻子中绽放，这不是由单一化合物而是由挥发性分子鸡尾酒创造的。没有单一的分子负责草莓的气味，而是一个家族——即呋喃酮（焦糖甜味）、甲基和乙基丁酸酯（果味）、甲基2-丙酸酯（果味）、己醛（绿色、尖锐）和顺-3-己烯醇（叶状清新）——蒸发并涌入鼻腔。

In each nostril, this medley of molecules dissolves into the mucus layer lining a 2.5 cm² patch of tissue known as the olfactory epithelium. Studded across this small region is a mosaic of roughly 10 million olfactory sensory neurons, each expressing only one type of olfactory receptor protein. In one breath, an odorant molecule, be it furaneol, cis-3-hexenol, or any other, activates a unique combination of receptors, similar to striking a subset of keys on a piano. This ensemble of activated neurons forms a distinct pattern that the brain reads as "strawberry."

在每个鼻孔中，这混合的分子溶解到覆盖着被称为嗅上皮的2.5平方厘米组织片的黏液层中。散布在这个小区域的是大约1000万个嗅觉感觉神经元的马赛克，每个只表达一种嗅觉受体蛋白。在一口气中，一个气味分子，无论是呋喃酮、顺-3-己烯醇还是其他，激活独特的受体组合，类似于敲击钢琴上的部分琴键。这组激活的神经元形成一个独特的模式，大脑将其解读为"草莓"。

Crucially, no two scents ever strike the same pattern. The combinatorial activity of hundreds of receptor genes allows humans to detect and discern more than a trillion distinct odors.

至关重要的是，没有两种气味会敲击相同的模式。数百个受体基因的组合活动使人类能够检测和辨别超过一万亿种不同的气味。

Each molecule activates a distinct set of odorant receptors.

每个分子激活一组独特的嗅觉受体。

While some olfactory receptors respond broadly, meaning they can recognize and bind to several different structurally-related molecules, other receptors are exquisitely selective and bind to only one specific shape or stereoisomer. For instance, the two mirror-twin forms of the organic compound carvone smell strikingly different — one like spearmint (R-carvone), the other like caraway (S-carvone) — underscoring the nuance the nose brings to discriminating between such molecular镜像 images.

虽然一些嗅觉受体广泛响应，意味着它们可以识别并结合几种不同的结构相关分子，但其他受体则非常选择性地只结合一种特定形状或立体异构体。例如，有机化合物香芹酮的两种镜像双胞胎形式闻起来明显不同——一种像留兰香（R-香芹酮），另一种像葛缕子（S-香芹酮）——这突显了鼻子在辨别这种分子镜像图像时的微妙之处。

The resultant signal, whether "caraway," "spearmint," or "strawberry," travels to the olfactory bulb, a bipartite nerve structure nestled at the base of the skull, just above the nasal passages. There, neurons expressing the same receptor type converge on specialized structures called glomeruli, the crucial processing hubs that sharpen and refine sensory input en route to deeper regions of the brain involved in odor discrimination.

产生的信号，无论是"葛缕子"、"留兰香"还是"草莓"，都传递到嗅球，这是一个位于颅底、鼻腔上方的双神经结构。在那里，表达相同受体类型的神经元汇聚到称为肾小球的专业结构上，这些关键的处理中心在通往大脑更深区域涉及气味辨别的途中锐化和提炼感觉输入。

Each glomerulus acts as a dedicated module for a single receptor type, receiving input from thousands of olfactory sensory neurons scattered throughout the nasal epithelium, but all tuned to the same molecular features. Within these spherical tangles of neural connections, the incoming signals undergo their first round of processing: they're amplified, filtered, and integrated by局部 interneurons that enhance contrast between different odor signals.

每个肾小球作为单一受体类型的专用模块，接收来自散布在整个鼻上皮的数千个嗅觉感觉神经元的输入，但都针对相同的分子特征。在这些球形神经连接缠结中，传入的信号经历第一轮处理：它们被局部中间神经元放大、过滤和整合，增强不同气味信号之间的对比度。

Though identical in atomic composition, the (R) and (S) enantiomers of carvone differ just enough in three-dimensional shape for olfactory detection. That slight stereochemical shift transforms perception itself: (R)-carvone reads as spearmint, while (S)-carvone evokes caraway.

尽管原子组成相同，香芹酮的(R)和(S)对映体在三维形状上的差异足以让嗅觉检测出来。这种轻微的立体化学转变改变了感知本身：(R)-香芹酮被感知为留兰香，而(S)-香芹酮唤起葛缕子的感觉。

Unlike other senses, these olfactory signals take an unusual route through the brain. While vision, hearing, and touch all pass through a brain region called the thalamus before reaching higher brain regions, smell bypasses this checkpoint. Instead, odor information travels directly from the olfactory bulb to the amygdala and hippocampus (two brain structures central to emotion and memory) before reaching conscious processing areas. This anatomical shortcut may explain why smells can trigger vivid memories and strong emotions before we've even consciously identified what we're smelling.

与其他感官不同，这些嗅觉信号在大脑中走一条不寻常的路径。虽然视觉、听觉和触觉在到达更高脑区之前都经过一个叫做丘脑的脑区，但嗅觉绕过了这个检查点。相反，气味信息直接从嗅球传递到杏仁核和海马体（两个对情绪和记忆至关重要的脑结构），然后才到达意识处理区域。这种解剖学上的捷径可能解释了为什么气味可以在我们甚至还没有意识到闻到什么之前就触发生动的记忆和强烈的情感。

Yet, while the nose has been anatomically mapped, receptors sequenced, and neural pathways charted, predicting a molecule's scent has remained a mysterious exercise. The question of why certain configurations of matter smell one way and not another persists. In other words, why does a molecule such as furaneol activate the receptor signaling "jammy sweetness"? What makes one molecule smell "grassy" and another "creamy," one trigger nausea and another nostalgia?

然而，虽然鼻子已经在解剖学上被绘制，受体被测序，神经通路被绘制成图，但预测分子的气味仍然是一项神秘的工作。为什么某些物质配置闻起来是一种方式而不是另一种方式的问题依然存在。换句话说，为什么像呋喃酮这样的分子会激活发出"果酱甜味"信号的受体？是什么让一个分子闻起来"草味"而另一个"奶油味"，一个引发恶心而另一个引发怀旧？

The prevailing hypothesis defines odorant molecules as ligands, specialized binding molecules that fit into olfactory receptors like a lock and key. This interaction activates the receptor, initiating an electrical response in the brain, a unique pattern of activity associated with a particular scent. While molecular structure长期以来 served as a proxy for predicting smell, particularly for researchers and香料化学家, the so-called Structure-Odor Relationship (SOR) paradox endures: molecules of nearly identical structure can smell worlds apart, while those with little in common can smell strikingly similar.

主流假设将气味分子定义为配体，专门适配到嗅觉受体中的结合分子，就像锁和钥匙一样。这种相互作用激活受体，在大脑中引发电反应，一种与特定气味相关的独特活动模式。虽然分子结构长期以来被用作预测气味的代理，特别是对研究人员和香料化学家而言，但所谓的结构-气味关系（SOR）悖论仍然存在：结构几乎相同的分子可能闻起来天差地别，而那些几乎没有共同点的分子可能闻起来惊人地相似。

Take musks, for instance: this family of compounds includes hundreds of molecules with vastly different structures and molecular weights, ranging from small macrocycles to large polycyclic frameworks. Yet, despite this diversity, many share a common warm, powdery, and animalic scent profile that defies straightforward structure-to-smell prediction.

以麝香为例：这个化合物家族包括数百种结构和分子量差异巨大的分子，从小型大环到大型多环框架。然而，尽管存在这种多样性，许多分子共享一种共同的温暖、粉状和动物性的气味特征，这违背了简单的结构到气味预测。

Muscone and civetone — both macrocyclic ketones with unique scaffolds — evoke the classic, animalic musk note. By contrast, nitro musks (e.g., musk xylene, musk ketone) rely on aromatic硝基-苯 scaffolds but still evoke the musky character associated with compounds like muscone and civetone. But odorants must also be volatile: molecules above ~300 Da, common among heavier musks, often lose perceptibility, becoming effectively odorless. These examples underscore how scent is shaped not only by molecular structure but also by chemical dynamics, receptor biology, and often a bit of mystery.

麝香酮和灵猫酮——都是具有独特支架的大环酮——唤起了经典的动物性麝香调。相比之下，硝基麝香（如二甲苯麝香、酮麝香）依赖于芳香硝基-苯支架，但仍唤起与麝香酮和灵猫酮等化合物相关的麝香特征。但气味剂也必须是挥发性的：超过约300 Da的分子，在较重的麝香中很常见，往往失去可感知性，变得实际上无味。这些例子强调了气味不仅由分子结构塑造，还由化学动力学、受体生物学以及往往一点神秘感塑造。

The speculation that chemical structure corresponds to scent dates back millennia, alongside the use of fragrant materials in ceremonial and cultural practice. From the early Greek atomists' theories of microscopic films emitted by objects to excite the senses, to the incense artisans of ancient Egypt and East Asia, humans have long sought to capture and understand the elusive nature of smell.

化学结构对应气味的推测可以追溯到千年前，伴随着芳香材料在仪式和文化实践中的使用。从早期希腊原子论者的物体发出微观薄膜刺激感官的理论，到古埃及和东亚的制香工匠，人类长期以来一直试图捕捉和理解难以捉摸的嗅觉本质。

Before the emergence of more sophisticated means for extracting and preserving scent, fragrance was elicited through rudimentary methods such as crushing raw botanicals, steeping them in oils, or burning resins to release their aroma.

在出现更复杂的提取和保存气味的方法之前，香味是通过粗糙的方法获得的，如压碎生植物、将其浸泡在油中，或燃烧树脂以释放其香气。

During the eighth and ninth centuries, the invention of the "modern" alembic — a distillation apparatus developed by Islamic alchemists featuring a still pot, a cooling condenser, and a collection spout — allowed for a more controlled extraction of delicate essential oils than crude crushing or simple heating could achieve. This innovation enabled new forms of fragrance production, introducing alcohol as a base and associating the practice of perf制造更 closely with chemistry and medicine. Through trade and translation, this technology and technique migrated to continental Europe, where perfumers expanded their repertoire over centuries with methods such as maceration, expression, tincturing, and enfleurage to isolate the volatile compounds of flowers and woods.

在八至九世纪，"现代"蒸馏器的发明——一种由伊斯兰炼金术士开发的蒸馏装置，具有蒸馏罐、冷却冷凝器和收集喷嘴——使得精细精油的提取比粗糙的压碎或简单的加热更能实现精确控制。这一创新使香水生产的新形式成为可能，引入酒精作为基础，并将香水制造实践与化学和医学更紧密地联系起来。通过贸易和翻译，这种技术和方法迁移到欧洲大陆，在那里，香水制造商在几个世纪里通过浸泡、压榨、酊剂和吸香等方法扩展了他们的曲目，以分离花卉和木材的挥发性化合物。

In the sixteenth century, tanners making leather gloves sought to mask the acrid smell of their wares with fragrant oils and extracts. Scented gloves quickly became fashionable among the European aristocracy, and perfumers found a lucrative market beyond functional necessity. At Versailles, the epicenter of French culture and royal power seat, courtly fashion demanded a complete sensory presentation: the right clothing, hairstyles, cosmetics, and an ever-changing repertoire of perfumed products to signal refinement and status.

在十六世纪，制作皮手套的制革工人试图用芳香油和提取物掩盖其商品的刺鼻气味。香氛手套迅速在欧洲贵族中流行起来，香水制造商在功能需求之外找到了一个利润丰厚的市场。在凡尔赛，法国文化和皇权的中心，宫廷时尚要求完整的感官呈现：合适的服装、发型、化妆品，以及不断变化的香氛产品曲目，以彰显精致和地位。

This high consumption of scent supported a nascent perfume industry, formalized in 1656 in Grasse, France, with the creation of the Maîtres Parfumeurs et Gantier (the perfume and glove-maker's guild). Alongside its favorable social-political milieu, Grasse's microclimate fostered a burgeoning perfume industry in the region, where fertile soils nurtured fragrant flowers such as jasmine, rose, lavender, orange blossom, myrtle, mimosa, and tuberose. This economic activity, along with the formation of local perfume, helped establish France's perfumeries as centers of both craft innovation and the formal study of smell.

这种高消费量支持了一个新兴的香水产业，于1656年在法国格拉斯随着香水和手套制造商公会的创建而正式确立。除了其有利的社会政治环境外，格拉斯的微气候促进了该地区蓬勃发展的香水产业，那里肥沃的土壤培育了茉莉、玫瑰、薰衣草、橙花、桃金娘、含羞草和晚香玉等芳香花卉。这种经济活动，连同当地香水的形成，帮助将法国的香水店确立为工艺创新和嗅觉正式研究的中心。

The nineteenth century marked a decisive shift as techniques from pharmacy and organic chemistry began to elucidate molecules underlying fragrance. Building on earlier breakthroughs such as the isolation of morphine in 1804 (the first alkaloid ever extracted from a plant), chemists turned their attention to芳香 materials.

十九世纪标志着一个决定性的转变，随着药学和有机化学技术开始阐明构成香味的分子。基于早期的突破，如1804年吗啡的分离（有史以来从植物中提取的第一种生物碱），化学家将注意力转向了芳香材料。

In 1820, the French pharmacist Nicolas Jean Baptiste Gaston Guibourt identified and isolated 2H-chromen-2-one, better known as coumarin, from the tonka bean (Dipteryx odorata). In an account presented to the pharmacy section of the Académie Royale de Médecine, Guibourt formally christened the new substance coumarine, marking one of the earliest instances of the chemical characterization of a fragrance compound. In 1858, another French chemist, Théodore Nicolas Gobley, succeeded in crystallizing vanillin from vanilla pods, confirming that odorants could be identified as discrete molecular entities.

1820年，法国药剂师尼古拉·让·巴蒂斯特·加斯顿·吉布尔从零陵香豆（Dipteryx odorata）中鉴定并分离出2H-色烯-2-酮，即更广为人知的香豆素。在向皇家医学院药学部提交的报告中，吉布尔正式为这个新物质命名为香豆素，标志着香味化合物化学特征化的最早实例之一。1858年，另一位法国化学家泰奥多尔·尼古拉·戈布莱成功从香草豆荚中结晶出香草醛，证实了气味剂可以被识别为离散的分子实体。

For the first time, individual aromatic molecules — a resonant note of the rose, one iota of the vanilla pod — could be extracted from a larger, complex composition. Smell was being recast as a phenomenon amenable to classification and design.

有史以来，单个芳香分子——玫瑰的一个共鸣音符，香草豆荚的一点点——可以从更大、更复杂的组合中提取出来。嗅觉正在被重塑为一种适合分类和设计的现象。

Work station of French perfumer Jean Carles - born in Grasse (1892), founded the Givaudan perfume school, one of the 'noses' behind the original 1947 edition of Miss Dior. Credit: Anna Kozlova

法国调香师让·卡尔的工作站——出生于格拉斯（1892年），创办了奇华顿香水学校，是1947年原版迪奥小姐背后的"鼻子"之一。致谢：Anna Kozlova

Driven by advancements in organic chemistry and the introduction of the periodic table in 1869, fragrance chemistry blossomed. By 1882, Paul Parquet's famous scent, Fougère Royale, featured coumarin, followed by Guerlain's Jicky in 1889, and in 1921, Chanel No. 5 launched using an entirely new class of synthetic compounds, the aldehydes. The perfume industry eagerly adopted new techniques, including chromatography and fractional distillation.

在有机化学的进步和1869年元素周期表的引入推动下，香料化学蓬勃发展。到1882年，保罗·帕尔凯的著名香水Fougère Royale使用了香豆素，随后是1889年娇兰的Jicky，1921年香奈儿5号使用了一种全新的合成化合物类别——醛类。香水行业热切地采用了新技术，包括色谱法和分馏法。

As the twentieth century unfolded, scientists intensified their quest for structural correlates of scent. Discoveries like ionone-related ketones evoking violet and woody notes, long-chain macrocycles recurring in musks, and aromatic rings anchoring vanillic and balsamic tones found their way to the traditional orgue à parfums, a semicircular, horseshoe-shaped work station: a harbinger of what would become a more严肃 investigation of the science and technology of smell.

随着二十世纪的发展，科学家加强了对气味结构相关性的探索。紫罗兰酮相关酮唤起紫罗兰和木质调、麝香中反复出现的长链大环、以及固定香草和香脂调的芳香环等发现，进入了传统的香水管风琴——一个半圆形、马蹄形的工作站：预示着对嗅觉科学和技术更严肃研究的到来。

Over eons, organisms evolved a sense of smell. In the past three centuries, smell became chemistry. And in recent years, we have started to ask whether smell can become code.

经过亿万年，生物进化出了嗅觉。在过去三个世纪里，嗅觉变成了化学。而在最近几年，我们开始问嗅觉是否能变成代码。

However, unlike sound and color, smell resists straightforward formalization. Color, for instance, has been reduced to three primary channels, standardized through the color wheel, and rendered into numeric systems that machines can interpret. Sound has been decomposed along perceptual axes of pitch, timbre, and amplitude, each capable of digital transformation. Odor, however, has no reliable relationship to molecular structure or perceptual encoding, making it difficult to establish a computational coordinate system.

然而，与声音和颜色不同，嗅觉难以直接形式化。例如，颜色已被简化为三个原色通道，通过色轮标准化，并转化为机器可以解读的数字系统。声音已被分解为音高、音色和振幅的感知轴，每个都能进行数字转换。然而，气味与分子结构或感知编码没有可靠的关系，使得建立计算坐标系统变得困难。

As a result, early computational efforts in representing molecules were often ad hoc: homemade tables to track atomic bonds, bespoke matrices, or custom linear codes that translated chemical structures into character strings. These methods虽然聪明 but small-scale and subjective, making calculations laborious and difficult to reproduce.

因此，早期在分子表示方面的计算工作往往是临时的：自制表格来跟踪原子键、定制矩阵，或将化学结构转化为字符串的自定义线性代码。这些方法虽然聪明，但规模小且主观，使计算变得费力且难以重现。

Then, in 1988, while at Pomona College in Claremont, California, chemist David Weininger devised SMILES (Simplified Molecular Input Line Entry System). SMILES provided a standardized, machine-readable line notation for encoding molecular structures. Like any language, SMILES is a formal system for representing information; in the context of chemistry, it acts like a cipher or code to translate molecular structures into linear character strings. A given molecule, according to convention, is spelled out as a word or a particular grammatical grouping of letters, where each letter corresponds to an individual atom. Contained in each word is also the logic for how certain letters connect or are arranged geometrically, including instructions for branching and ring closures. For instance, the six-carbon ring cyclohexane is represented by slicing open the ring and labeling the adjacent atoms with matching numbers to indicate connection and closure: C1CCCCC1. In the same fashion, coumarin can be written as O=C1OC2=CC=CC=C2C=C1.

然后，1988年，在加利福尼亚州克莱蒙特的波莫纳学院，化学家大卫·温宁格设计了SMILES（简化分子输入线性输入系统）。SMILES提供了一种标准化的、机器可读的线性表示法来编码分子结构。像任何语言一样，SMILES是一种表示信息的形式系统；在化学背景下，它就像一个密码或代码，将分子结构转化为线性字符串。按照惯例，给定分子被拼写为一个单词或特定的字母语法组合，其中每个字母对应一个单独的原子。每个单词中还包含某些字母如何连接或几何排列的逻辑，包括分支和环闭合的指令。例如，六碳环环己烷通过切开环并用匹配的数字标记相邻原子来表示连接和闭合：C1CCCCC1。同样，香豆素可以写成O=C1OC2=CC=CC=C2C=C1。

In practice, however, this descriptor-based model could not contend with the idiosyncratic nature of the structure-to-odor relationship: a molecule may be classified as citrus-like by virtue of its ester group, while a structurally analogous compound with only minor modifications might be seen as sharp, metallic, or entirely odorless.

然而，在实践中，这种基于描述符的模型无法应对结构到气味关系的特殊性：一个分子可能因其酯基而被归类为柑橘类，而结构上类似的化合物只有微小的修改可能被视为尖锐、金属味或完全无味的。

As academic labs continued researching the logic of scent, focus shifted toward building larger datasets that linked molecular structure to qualitative descriptions of odor perception. In place of binary quantifiers such as odorous/odorless or pleasant/unpleasant, these collections captured distinct attributes like fruity, floral, musky, or burnt, offering a more embodied and qualitative relationship between chemistry and体验.

随着学术实验室继续研究嗅觉逻辑，重点转向建立更大的数据集，将分子结构与气味感知的定性描述联系起来。代替有气味/无味或愉悦/不愉悦的二元量化，这些收集捕捉了果味、花香、麝香或焦糊等独特属性，提供了化学与体验之间更具实体性和定性的关系。

Democratizing databases such as the Good Scents Company, for example, expanded the vocabulary of olfaction available for computation, priming the field for machine learning approaches capable of finding patterns across large, multidimensional datasets.

例如，像Good Scents Company这样的民主化数据库扩展了可用于计算的嗅觉词汇，为能够在大型多维数据集中发现模式的机器学习方法奠定了基础。

In 2015, IBM Research and Rockefeller University launched the DREAM Olfaction Prediction Challenge, the first large-scale, open benchmark for predicting human olfactory perception from the physicochemical features of odor molecules. Researchers were provided with data comprising 476 chemically diverse odorants, each described by over 4,800 molecular features and rated by 49 human participants on 19 sensory attributes, such as sweet, fish, mint, and sour.

2015年，IBM研究院和洛克菲勒大学发起了DREAM嗅觉预测挑战赛，这是第一个大规模、开放的基准测试，用于从气味分子的物理化学特征预测人类嗅觉感知。研究人员获得的数据包括476种化学多样性气味剂，每种由超过4,800个分子特征描述，并由49名人类参与者在19个感官属性上进行评分，如甜、鱼、薄荷和酸。

Competing teams trained machine-learning models to map feature-perception relationships and evaluated their models using 69 unseen test molecules. The top models achieved prediction measures comparable to those observed among human participants, meaning the machine ratings were nearly as reliable as人类鼻子 when judging whether a chemical smelled floral or fishy. The models' predictions mirrored the average agreement reported in human odor-perception ratings, demonstrating accuracy of up to 85 percent across multiple different aromas.

竞争团队训练机器学习模型来绘制特征-感知关系，并使用69个未见的测试分子评估他们的模型。顶级模型达到了与人类参与者相当的预测指标，意味着机器评分在判断化学物质闻起来是花香还是鱼腥味时几乎和人类鼻子一样可靠。模型的预测反映了人类气味感知评分中报告的平均一致性，在多种不同香气中展示了高达85%的准确率。

Although the DREAM challenge yielded compelling evidence that odor perception could be forecasted computationally, it had its shortcomings. Namely, the study's dataset remained modest in scale, leaving vast chemical territories uncharted, and its reliance on predefined molecular descriptors meant the models depended on prescribed features rather than uncovering the latent logic linking structure to odor.

尽管DREAM挑战产生了令人信服的证据，表明气味感知可以通过计算预测，但它也有其缺点。具体来说，研究的数据集规模仍然有限，留下广阔的化学领域未被探索，而且它对预定义分子描述符的依赖意味着模型依赖于规定的特征，而不是揭示连接结构与气味的潜在逻辑。

By 2019, researchers at Google Brain (now DeepMind) sought to overcome these constraints by expanding both the scale of training data and the fidelity of their model. Using deep learning and Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), a class of models that interpret molecules as graphs with atoms and bonds represented as nodes and edges, respectively, the study employed a dataset containing more than 5,000 odorants annotated by expert perfumers across over 100 descriptors, from mint and potato to sulfurous and grassy.

到2019年，谷歌大脑（现为DeepMind）的研究人员试图通过扩大训练数据的规模和模型的保真度来克服这些限制。使用深度学习和图神经网络（GNN），一类将分子解释为图的模型，其中原子和键分别表示为节点和边，该研究使用了一个包含超过5,000种气味剂的数据集，由专业调香师在超过100个描述符上进行注释，从薄荷和土豆到硫磺和青草。

Unlike the earlier DREAM Challenge, which relied on prescribed molecular features, such as atom counts and topological indices, this approach allowed the model to直接从数据推断结构-气味关系。To initiate the training process, each node (representing an atom) was seeded with basic chemical information, including atomic number、价态、杂化和形式电荷。

与早期依赖规定分子特征（如原子计数和拓扑指数）的DREAM挑战不同，这种方法允许模型直接从数据推断结构-气味关系。为了启动训练过程，每个节点（代表一个原子）被赋予基本的化学信息，包括原子序数、价态、杂化和形式电荷。

Through a recursive process known as "message passing," the state of each atom was repeatedly updated in response to information relayed by neighboring nodes. Layer by layer, the model built up a progressively bigger picture of a molecule: early layers learned to identify local特征 like carbonyls、卤化物、或 ring systems, while deeper layers learned to recognize broader chemical motifs such as芳香性、共轭（p轨道的重叠）和立体应变（由于非直接键合原子之间的电子排斥导致分子势能增加）。

通过一个被称为"消息传递"的递归过程，每个原子的状态根据邻近节点传递的信息反复更新。一层又一层，模型逐步构建出分子的更大图景：早期层学习识别局部特征如羰基、卤化物或环系统，而更深层的层学习识别更广泛的化学基序，如芳香性、共轭（p轨道的重叠）和立体应变（由于非直接键合原子之间的电子排斥导致分子势能增加）。

By the penultimate layer, the complete, networked "picture" of a molecule was condensed into a fixed-length, 63-dimensional vector, known as an "odor embedding," capturing the molecule's perceptual qualities in a computable form. This embedding was then used for classification tasks, such as predicting specific odor描述符 or categorizing the molecule's scent profile.

到倒数第二层，分子的完整网络"图像"被浓缩成一个固定长度的63维向量，被称为"气味嵌入"，以可计算的形式捕捉分子的感知品质。然后，这个嵌入被用于分类任务，如预测特定气味描述符或分类分子的气味特征。

When these "images" were compressed, molecules that smelled alike clustered together even when their chemical scaffolds were structurally dissimilar. For example, certain esters和 ketones, molecules that share little in common structurally, appeared close in this space because both carried a sweet, fruity scent, while structurally related compounds could be separated if their perceived odors diverged. This act of compression thus exposed a "geometry of smell."

当这些"图像"被压缩时，闻起来相似的分子聚集在一起，即使它们的化学支架结构不同。例如，某些酯和酮，结构上几乎没有共同点的分子，在这个空间中显得接近，因为两者都带有甜美、果香的气味，而如果感知气味不同，结构相关的化合物可能被分开。这种压缩行为因此揭示了一种"气味的几何学"。

It also laid the groundwork for Google's subsequent introduction of the Principal Odor Map (POM): the first map of smell, in which traversing through space corresponds to crossing distinct regions of aroma, from jasmine to potato. Building on the previous study, the researchers expanded the model's embedding layer by 193 dimensions, creating a high-dimensional coordinate system that positions each odorous molecule as a single point within a continuous manifold (e.g., a connected shape or space that behaves geometrically, enabling meaningful comparison and relation between the position of points representing molecules). In this space, perceptually similar odors occupy nearby positions, much as blue lies closer to turquoise than to crimson on the color轮, while increasingly dissimilar ones are separated by greater distance.

它也为谷歌随后引入主气味图（POM）奠定了基础：第一张气味地图，在其中穿越空间对应于跨越不同的香气区域，从茉莉到土豆。基于之前的研究，研究人员将模型的嵌入层扩展了193维，创建了一个高维坐标系统，将每个有气味的分子定位为一个连续流形内的单一点（例如，一个几何行为的连接形状或空间，使得代表分子的点位置之间可以进行有意义的比较和关联）。在这个空间中，感知相似的气味占据相近的位置，就像蓝色在色轮上比深红色更接近绿松石色，而越来越不相似的气味则被更大的距离分开。

When the researchers subjected the POM to further tests, they found that it could generalize beyond human olfactory perception, predicting olfactory receptor activity across species from mice to insects. This cross-species application suggests that the model's internal structure even captures evolutionary principles of scent. On the map itself, metabolically related compounds — those separated by only a few metabolic reactions or naturally共现于 the same substance — also occupy nearby coordinates, forming concentrated clusters that reflect their shared chemical and perceptual relationships.

当研究人员对POM进行进一步测试时，他们发现它可以推广到人类嗅觉感知之外，预测从老鼠到昆虫等物种的嗅觉受体活动。这种跨物种应用表明，模型的内部结构甚至捕捉了气味的进化原理。在地图本身上，代谢相关的化合物——那些只被几个代谢反应分离或自然共现于同一物质的化合物——也占据相近的坐标，形成反映其共享化学和感知关系的集中聚类。

By capturing such subtle perceptual relationships, the model not only surpassed traditional chemoinformatic algorithms in terms of predictive accuracy, but also enabled early steps toward the design of new odorants.

通过捕捉这种微妙的感知关系，该模型不仅在预测准确性方面超越了传统的化学信息学算法，还为新气味剂的设计迈出了早期步伐。

Building on this foundation, Osmo, a start-up spun out of Google Research in 2022, has begun leveraging its proprietary Olfactory Intelligence (OI) platform informed by POM to translate sensory scent data into chemical design. The company's goal is to "give computers a sense of smell."

在此基础上，Osmo——一家于2022年从谷歌研究院分拆出来的初创公司——开始利用其由POM提供支持的专有嗅觉智能（OI）平台，将感官气味数据转化为化学设计。该公司的目标是"让计算机拥有嗅觉"。

Glossine, Fractaline, and Quasarine are the first prompt-generated fragrance molecules created using its platform. According to the company, Glossine delivers a "Las Vegas-style sparkle," with a floral jasmine-like top note that adheres well to textiles; while Fractaline offers a versatile violet note, reputed to give a powdery, "second-skin" impression; and Quasarine presents an intense yet delicate jasmine aroma claimed to deliver a long-lasting "fresh petal-y effect." While these descriptors could be shared by other perfumes, these fragrances are entirely original. Where traditional perfumery relies on the discovery and refinement of proprietary molecules in a process that usually takes years, Osmo can "create" completely new odorants through this OI platform in just a few days.

Glossine、Fractaline和Quasarine是使用其平台创建的第一批提示生成的香料分子。据该公司称，Glossine带来"拉斯维加斯风格的闪耀"，具有类似茉莉的花香前调，能很好地附着于纺织品；而Fractaline提供多功能紫罗兰调，据称给人粉状的"第二层皮肤"印象；Quasarine呈现强烈而精致的茉莉香气，据称能提供持久的"新鲜花瓣效果"。虽然这些描述符可能被其他香水共享，但这些香味是完全原创的。传统香水制造依赖于发现和精炼专有分子的过程，通常需要数年，而Osmo可以通过这个OI平台在短短几天内"创造"全新的气味剂。

The potential applications of Osmo's OI platform, however, extend far beyond fragrance. Because the Principal Odor Map can extrapolate olfaction across species, Osmo has leveraged it for developing synthetic alternatives to DEET, the active ingredient in most商业 insect repellents, but one which has been linked to adverse effects, including severe skin reactions like rashes and welts. Trained on experimental datasets measuring mosquito repellency, the model can predict the repellent efficacy of nearly any molecule. Experimental validation confirmed over a dozen new molecules as repellent as DEET, but which could offer safer, longer-lasting alternatives for killing mosquitoes and ticks.

然而，Osmo的OI平台的潜在应用远远超出了香水领域。因为主气味图可以跨物种推断嗅觉，Osmo已将其用于开发DEET的合成替代品，DEET是大多数商业驱虫剂中的活性成分，但它与包括严重皮肤反应如皮疹和荨麻疹等不良影响有关。在测量驱蚊效果的实验数据集上训练后，该模型可以预测几乎任何分子的驱虫效果。实验验证确认了超过十几种新分子与DEET一样具有驱虫效果，但可以为杀灭蚊子和蜱虫提供更安全、更持久的替代品。

Beyond repellents, Osmo has broader ambitions to also develop non-invasive diagnostic tools. They aim, for example, to identify volatile signatures emitted by various diseases, enabling algorithms with a "digital nose" to detect conditions such as Parkinson's、糖尿病、and certain cancers through subtle changes in body odor or sebum, a type of signal detectable by dogs.

除了驱虫剂，Osmo还有更广泛的雄心，开发非侵入性诊断工具。例如，他们的目标是识别各种疾病散发的挥发性特征，使具有"数字鼻子"的算法能够通过体味或皮脂的细微变化检测帕金森病、糖尿病和某些癌症，这是一种狗可以检测到的信号。

Perhaps the most promising application of Osmo's technology, though, relates to sustainability. By synthetically generating fragrance molecules, Osmo offers a path toward decreasing the environmental impact of industrial scent-production. A canonical and increasingly endangered raw material for scents is rose, whose odor has captivated humans for millennia yet remains one of the most labor-intensive and expensive natural ingredients (some 60,000 roses, roughly 180 kilograms of petals, are required to produce a single ounce of oil, which sells for $8,000 to $12,000 per kilogram). By plotting the scent of Rosa damascena and Rosa centifolia on the POM, Osmo can work toward "reverse-engineering" the rose and other floral notes in pursuit of designing "an alternate universe of perfume ingredients" that are perceived similarly without requiring raw botanical sources.

然而，Osmo技术最有前景的应用可能与可持续性有关。通过合成生成香料分子，Osmo提供了一条减少工业气味生产环境影响的道路。玫瑰是香味的经典且日益濒危的原材料，其气味千百年来一直吸引着人类，但仍是最劳动密集型和昂贵的天然成分之一（大约需要60,000朵玫瑰，约180公斤花瓣，才能生产一盎司油，每公斤售价8,000至12,000美元）。通过在POM上绘制大马士革玫瑰和百叶玫瑰的气味，Osmo可以努力"逆向工程"玫瑰和其他花香调，追求设计"香水成分的平行宇宙"，这些成分被类似地感知而不需要原始植物来源。

Osmo is not alone in its effort to advance computationally mediated scent. Givaudan's Carto, the digital design platform of one of the world's first and largest fragrance houses, is also using computer technology to design its perfumes. Carto integrates predictive algorithms with a database of over 5,000 raw materials (five times more than a human perfumer typically manages) to help perfumers simulate、match、and modify scents. Whereas the classical perfume orgue à parfums once arranged rows of essences within arm's reach, Carto replaces physical glass vials with a virtual glass touchscreen display. The platform was used to formulate Tom Ford's Bois Pacifique, which launched in January 2025.

Osmo并非独自努力推进计算介导的嗅觉。奇华顿的Carto是世界上第一家也是最大的香水公司的数字设计平台，也在使用计算机技术设计其香水。Carto将预测算法与超过5,000种原材料的数据库（是人类调香师通常管理数量的五倍）相结合，帮助调香师模拟、匹配和修改气味。虽然古典的香水管风琴曾经将成排的精华放在触手可及的地方，但Carto用虚拟玻璃触摸屏显示器取代了物理玻璃瓶。该平台被用于配制汤姆·福特的Bois Pacifique，于2025年1月推出。

Despite such recent successes, the mysteries of olfaction are far from solved. To digitize the scent of a rose, for instance, requires contending with over 300 volatile compounds that contribute to its spicy、蜂蜜般的、and tea-like notes. These compounds are not simply additive but dynamic; their interactions、相对浓度、and release create emergent qualities unpredictable from any single component alone. While platforms like Osmo and Carto can tinker with individual molecules, capturing the interplay of dozens or hundreds of odorants in a given mixture remains a formidable challenge, the mastery of which will be next in digitizing smell.

尽管最近取得了这些成功，嗅觉的奥秘远未解开。例如，要数字化玫瑰的气味，需要应对超过300种挥发性化合物，这些化合物贡献了其辛辣、蜂蜜和茶香的调性。这些化合物不仅仅是叠加的，而是动态的；它们的相互作用、相对浓度和释放创造了从任何单一成分单独无法预测的突现品质。虽然像Osmo和Carto这样的平台可以摆弄单个分子，但捕捉给定混合物中数十或数百种气味剂的相互作用仍然是一个艰巨的挑战，掌握这一点将是数字化嗅觉的下一步。

Both industry and academia continue to advance this aim. The most recent DREAM Olfaction Challenge, slated to close in late 2025, tasked international teams with predicting perceptual similarity across volatile mixtures. It will draw on a dataset of over 700 combinations containing anywhere from two to ten different molecules to assess how accurately teams can digitally model smells、their interactions、and their resulting impressions. Although peer-reviewed results have not yet been published, early findings point toward improvements in complex-mixture scent prediction.

业界和学术界都在继续推进这一目标。最新的DREAM嗅觉挑战赛定于2025年底结束，要求国际团队预测挥发性混合物之间的感知相似性。它将利用超过700种组合的数据集，每种组合包含两到十种不同的分子，来评估团队能以多高的精度数字化模拟气味、它们的相互作用以及由此产生的印象。虽然同行评审的结果尚未发表，但早期发现指向复杂混合物气味预测的改进。

Traditionally, the fragrance industry has made a point of drawing a line between the natural and the synthetic, with the introduction of synthetic scent molecules in the mid-nineteenth century garnering both criticism and celebration. Early critics proclaimed such synthetic materials as "vulgar debasements" of perfumery, reflecting anxieties surrounding industrialization and失去遗产和工艺的焦虑。But others celebrated this innovation. Guerlain's Jicky (1889), for instance, which incorporated synthetic vanillin and coumarin, was hailed as a new chapter for perfume. Moreover, commercial houses like Lubin marketed synthetic musks as "triumphs of science … over Nature," promoting the "non-evanescence" (long-lasting) qualities of synthetic chemicals in comparison to天然成分。

传统上，香水行业一直强调天然与合成之间的界限，十九世纪中期合成气味分子的引入既受到批评也受到庆祝。早期批评家宣称这种合成材料是对香水制造的"粗俗贬低"，反映了对工业化和失去遗产与工艺的焦虑。但其他人庆祝这一创新。例如，娇兰的Jicky（1889年），使用了合成香草醛和香豆素，被誉为香水的新篇章。此外，像Lubin这样的商业公司将合成麝香推销为"科学对自然的胜利"，宣传合成化学品的"非挥发性"（持久）品质，与天然成分相比。

These developments unfolded within a wider cultural moment that the essayist Max Beerbohm described as "a new epoch of artifice." The emergence of synthetics did not simply replace the natural; it upset the very idea of what "naturalness" meant — did it refer to an ingredient's source、its sensory impression、or the meanings attached to it?

这些发展在一个更广泛的文化时刻展开，散文家马克斯·比尔博姆将其描述为"人工的新纪元"。合成物的出现不仅仅是取代了天然；它颠覆了"天然性"本身的概念——它指的是成分的来源、其感官印象，还是附属于它的意义？

Even today, materials such as ambergris (a waxy、甜味的物质 formed in the digestive system of sperm whales and expelled into the ocean) remain highly coveted precisely because of their scarcity and natural origins. Revered as "the treasure of the sea," ambergris is found in less than five percent of sperm whales and develops its celebrated complexity over years of exposure to sea、salt、and sun. Such a valued ingredient commands reverence precisely because of its rarity, exposing how ideas of purity and authenticity continue to shape our perception and appreciation. Ultimately, the persisting popularity of ingredients such as ambergris demonstrates that value pertains as much to culture as to chemistry, reflecting not only the reality of raw materials but also the moral and aesthetic weight we ascribe to their来源。

即使在今天，像龙涎香（一种在抹香鲸消化系统中形成并排入海中的蜡状甜味物质）这样的材料仍然因其稀缺性和天然来源而备受追捧。被尊为"海洋之宝"的龙涎香在不到5%的抹香鲸中发现，并经过数年海水、盐和阳光的暴露才发展出其著名的复杂性。这种珍贵的成分之所以受到崇敬，恰恰是因为其稀有性，揭示了纯真和真实性的观念如何继续塑造我们的感知和欣赏。最终，龙涎香等成分的持续流行表明，价值与文化有关，也与化学有关，不仅反映原材料的现实，还反映我们赋予其来源的道德和审美权重。

Given this legacy, how might we relate to machine-generated molecules?

鉴于这一传统，我们如何看待机器生成的分子？

The shift to digitized scent will require that we rethink not only the substances themselves, but also the stories we tell as we find ways to valorize their synthetic origins. Such computationally-designed scents are, after all, safer、过敏原友好的配方 compliant with evolving regulations. Furthermore, AI-designed aromas often use more sustainably produced molecules, with a lab-created civetone replacing wild civet extract (a substance associated with the questionable practice of keeping civet cats in captivity for their perineal gland secretions).

向数字化嗅觉的转变将要求我们不仅重新思考物质本身，还要重新思考我们在寻找方法来重视其合成来源时讲述的故事。毕竟，这种计算设计的香味是更安全、符合不断演变法规的过敏原友好配方。此外，人工智能设计的香气通常使用更可持续生产的分子，用实验室创建的香猫酮替代野生香猫提取物（一种与将有问题的将香猫圈养以获取其会阴腺分泌物相关的物质）。

A similar parallel has played out elsewhere: a one-carat lab-grown diamond, while chemically and optically identical to a mined one, can cost less than a quarter of the price, despite being purer and more ethically produced. While they have been derided as "costume jewelry," the market for lab-grown diamonds was valued at more than $18 billion in 2023 and is expected to continue growing. The connotations of luxury are increasingly leaning toward narratives of sustainable and ethical sourcing.

类似的平行情况在其他地方也有发生：一颗一克拉的实验室培育钻石，虽然在化学和光学上与开采的钻石相同，但价格不到其四分之一，尽管更纯净且更具道德性。虽然它们被嘲笑为"仿造珠宝"，但实验室培育钻石的市场在2023年价值超过180亿美元，预计将继续增长。奢华的内涵越来越倾向于可持续和道德采购的叙事。

Machine-imagined scents may follow a similar arc. But just as the telescope widened our aperture to distant cosmic structures, digitally composed scents will expand our olfactory range, this time towards the smallest scales of chemical configuration.

机器想象的气味可能遵循类似的轨迹。但正如望远镜拓宽了我们对遥远宇宙结构的视野，数字化合成的气味将扩展我们的嗅觉范围，这次朝向化学配置的最小尺度。

## 批判性思考评论

### 1. 技术进步的两面性

这篇文章展示了AI在嗅觉数字化方面的令人印象深刻的进展，但我们也需要思考：当我们能够用算法"创造"气味时，嗅觉的神秘性和主观性是否正在被过度简化？嗅觉不仅仅是分子与受体的机械互动，它还承载着文化记忆、个人经历和情感联结。POM（主气味图）虽然在预测能力上取得了突破，但它能否真正捕捉到气味的"灵魂"——那种唤起的回忆和情感反应？

### 2. 天然vs合成的伦理困境

文章巧妙地通过龙涎香和实验室培育钻石的类比，揭示了消费者对"天然性"的执着可能更多是文化和心理建构而非理性选择。然而，这种类比也存在问题：龙涎香的形成涉及海洋生物数十年的海洋漂流和自然化学转化，这种"时间价值"和"不可复制性"是实验室无法模拟的。完全拥抱合成是否会让我们失去与自然世界的某种深层联系？

### 3. 可持续发展的悖论

Osmo等平台声称通过合成香料减少对濒危植物（如玫瑰）的依赖，这听起来是环保的。但我们需要审视：生产这些合成分子的能源消耗、化学废物处理、以及AI计算本身的碳足迹是否真正比传统种植更可持续？文章中提到的"60,000朵玫瑰生产一盎司油"虽然强调了天然资源的稀缺，但也凸显了传统香水产业本身就是极度资源密集型的——问题可能不在于天然vs合成，而在于整个消费模式的反思。

### 4. 嗅觉民主化的隐忧

当AI可以预测和设计气味时，谁掌握了这种技术的控制权？大型科技公司和香水巨头可能会垄断"气味的未来"。文章提到Carto平台管理着5,000种原材料，是人类调香师的五倍——这种能力的不对称是否会导致传统调香技艺的消亡？我们需要警惕技术垄断对文化多样性的侵蚀。

### 5. 跨物种嗅觉的伦理边界

POM能够预测从老鼠到昆虫的嗅觉受体活动，这种跨物种能力虽然令人兴奋，但也引发了关于动物实验和生物操纵的伦理问题。如果我们能设计出针对特定物种的驱虫剂，我们是否也在创造针对特定物种的"化学武器"？技术的中立性需要持续的伦理审视。

### 6. 嗅觉主观性的挑战

文章提到个体之间只有90种受体是普遍存在的，356种受体中有77%在任何时候都在表达，这种巨大的个体差异意味着"气味地图"可能永远只能是统计学上的平均，而非普适真理。AI模型的高准确率（85%）听起来很高，但对于那15%的"异常"感知者呢？他们的嗅觉体验是否会被边缘化？

### 总结

这篇文章为我们展示了嗅觉科学的前沿进展，但更重要的是，它提醒我们技术进步从来不是纯粹的技术问题。当我们谈论"数字化嗅觉"时，我们实际上在谈论如何重新定义人类与自然、传统与创新、个体与集体之间的关系。在拥抱这些激动人心的可能性时，保持批判性思维和伦理警惕同样重要。
