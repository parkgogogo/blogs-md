## English Content

This technical deep-dive explores building the smallest possible transformer model capable of adding two 10-digit numbers with over 99% accuracy.

Key learnings:
- Fundamental transformer mechanics at minimal scale
- Optimization techniques for small models
- How neural networks handle arithmetic operations
- Insights into model capacity vs task complexity

The project demonstrates:
- Minimal viable architecture for arithmetic tasks
- Training strategies for precise calculations
- The boundary of what small transformers can achieve
