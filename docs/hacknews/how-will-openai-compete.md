# How Will OpenAI Compete?
# OpenAI 将如何应对竞争？

**Source:** https://www.ben-evans.com/benedictevans/2026/2/19/how-will-openai-compete-nkg2x  
**Date:** 2026-02-25

Benedict Evans analyzes the competitive landscape facing OpenAI as the AI industry matures and differentiation becomes increasingly difficult. The article examines strategic options for maintaining market leadership in a rapidly commoditizing field.

Benedict Evans 分析了 OpenAI 面临的竞争格局，随着 AI 行业成熟和差异化变得越来越困难。文章考察了在一个快速商品化的领域中保持市场领导地位的战略选择。

The analysis suggests that OpenAI's early mover advantage is eroding as competitors like Anthropic, Google, and open-source alternatives reach parity on core capabilities. This convergence raises questions about sustainable competitive moats in the AI industry.

分析认为，随着 Anthropic、Google 和开源替代品在核心能力上达到同等水平，OpenAI 的先发优势正在侵蚀。这种融合引发了关于 AI 行业可持续竞争护城河的问题。

Key strategic challenges identified:
- Model capabilities are becoming commoditized
- Inference costs continue to decline rapidly
- Enterprise customers demand flexibility across providers
- Regulatory pressures increase operational complexity

识别的关键战略挑战：
- 模型能力正在商品化
- 推理成本持续快速下降
- 企业客户要求跨提供商的灵活性
- 监管压力增加运营复杂性

Evans argues that OpenAI's path forward likely involves deepening integration into enterprise workflows rather than competing purely on model performance. The company's partnership with Microsoft and focus on developer tools represents a strategic pivot toward platform dominance.

Evans 认为，OpenAI 的前进道路可能涉及深化与企业工作流的集成，而非纯粹在模型性能上竞争。该公司与微软的合作以及对开发者工具的关注代表着向平台主导权的战略转向。

The article also explores the role of open-source models in commoditizing the market, noting that Llama, DeepSeek, and other open weights models are achieving competitive performance while eliminating inference costs entirely for self-hosted deployments.

文章还探讨了开源模型在商品化市场中的作用，指出 Llama、DeepSeek 和其他开源权重模型正在实现有竞争力的性能，同时完全消除了自托管部署的推理成本。

Ultimately, Evans suggests that the winning strategy may not be building the best model, but rather creating the most valuable ecosystem around AI capabilities—a lesson from previous technology platform shifts.

最终，Evans 认为获胜的策略可能不是构建最好的模型，而是围绕 AI 能力创造最有价值的生态系统——这是以往技术平台转变的教训。
