# 处理 Antigravity 封禁及恢复访问

**Original:** https://github.com/google-gemini/gemini-cli/discussions/20632  
**Rating:** 8/10 | **Category:** AI

---

## English Content

Google's official response to mass bans affecting OpenClaw users on their Antigravity AI coding platform.

Key points:
- Varun Mohan cited "massive increase in malicious usage" degrading service quality
- Appeal process available for automatic reinstatement
- ToS violations clarified related to third-party tool usage
- Many OpenClaw users were affected by the bans

The incident highlights the ongoing tension between:
- AI platform security and accessibility
- Third-party tool developers and platform providers
- Automated usage detection and legitimate use cases

## 中文翻译

Google 官方回应 Antigravity 平台大规模封禁 OpenClaw 用户事件。

要点：
- Varun Mohan 表示检测到"恶意使用激增"导致服务质量下降
- 提供申诉流程以实现自动恢复
- 澄清了与第三方工具使用相关的服务条款违规
- 许多 OpenClaw 用户受到封禁影响

该事件突显了以下之间的持续紧张关系：
- AI 平台安全性与可访问性
- 第三方工具开发者与平台提供商
- 自动使用检测与合法用例
