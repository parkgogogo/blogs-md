## English Content

Gary Marcus alleges corruption in AI government contracts.

Key allegations:
- Sam Altman secretly worked on a Pentagon deal
- This happened while publicly supporting Anthropic's Dario Amodei
- Political donations may have influenced AI procurement decisions
- Concerns about cronyism in the emerging AI industry

The article raises important questions about:
- Transparency in AI government contracts
- Relationships between AI companies and defense sector
- Ethics of AI development for military use
